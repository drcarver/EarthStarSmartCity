# Multispectral and VOC Sensors  
**Genesis Network Environmental Monitoring Component**

**Multispectral and VOC sensors** are critical instruments in the Genesis Network's **[dense sensor nets](../components/dense-sensor-nets.md)**, enabling real-time detection of biodiversity indicators, air quality parameters, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and pollution sources. These sensors support compliance with **ISO 37120** (city services and quality of life) and **ISO 37123** (resilience indicators), particularly in environment, health, and safety categories.

## Overview and Technical Specifications

**Multispectral sensors** capture data across 5–20 spectral bands (typically 400–2500 nm), providing high-resolution imagery for vegetation health, soil composition, water quality, and pest/disease early warning. **VOC sensors** detect organic compounds (e.g., methane, benzene, formaldehyde) at ppm/ppb levels, identifying emissions from industrial processes, wildfires, or biological activity.

- **Integration**: Embedded in **[toughened ceramic tiles](../components/tiles-catalog.md)** (3–5× redundancy), **[aquaponics pods](../components/pods/aquaponics-pods.md)**, **[livestock pods](../components/pods/livestock-pods.md)**, and **[rewilding corridors](../strategy/rewilding-corridors.md)**.  
- **Power**: Self-powered via **[piezoelectric harvesting](../components/piezoelectric-harvesting.md)** and **[BIPV glazing](../components/bipv-glazing.md)**; edge AI processing on **[RISC-V compute](../components/risc-v-compute.md)**.  
- **Data Flow**: Feeds directly into the **[data moat](../docs/data-moat.md)** for ruthless optimization and predictive analytics.  
- **Standards Compliance**: **[IEEE 1451](../docs/standards/ieee-1451.md)** (transducer interfaces), **[OGC SWE](../docs/standards/ogc-swe.md)** (sensor web enablement), **[ITU-T Y.4900](../docs/standards/itu-t-y4900.md)** (smart city KPIs).  

## Applications in the Genesis Network

1. **Biodiversity Monitoring**  
   Multispectral imaging tracks NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index), species composition, and habitat health in **[blue-green carbon kits](../components/blue-green-carbon-kits.md)** and **[silvopasture](../strategy/silvopasture-integration.md)** zones.

2. **Air Quality & Pollution Detection**  
   VOC sensors monitor emissions from **[biorefinery pods](../components/pods/biorefinery-pods.md)**, wildfires, or external sources; integrated with **[smart airlocks](../components/smart-airlock-security.md)** for anomaly alerts.

3. **Restoration Corridors & Aquaponics Pods**  
   Deployed in **[rewilding](../strategy/rewilding-sustainable-forestry.md)** areas for soil/plant stress detection; in **[multi-trophic aquaponics](../components/multi-trophic-aquaponics.md)** for nutrient/pH cross-validation.

4. **Ecology Studies & Virtual Experiences**  
   Dense deployment enables PB-scale data for global research; feeds **[telepresence hubs](../components/telepresence-hubs.md)** for virtual vacations and holographic forest tours.
