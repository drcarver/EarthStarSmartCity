## Executive Overview

BVOC & Microbial Sensors LLC develops and produces advanced biogenic volatile organic compound (BVOC) and microbial activity sensors for integration into the Genesis Network’s data moat. These sensors enable real-time monitoring of plant stress, soil microbial health, pathogen detection, and atmospheric VOCs, driving ruthless optimization of yields, sequestration, and closed-loop efficiency. Deployed across tiles, pods, and restoration zones, they compound the Network’s 10–50× experimental advantage. Revenue comes from internal transfer pricing, premium kit sales to affiliates, and licensing for compliance/credits. Pilot integration begins 2028–2030; mature scale by 2035+; contributes $150–450M/hub/year in value (yield boosts + credits).
