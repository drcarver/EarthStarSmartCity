# Pre-Supply Staging  
**Genesis Network Acceleration Mechanism**  
**Last Updated: January 21, 2026**

## Overview

**Pre-supply staging** is the single most powerful acceleration lever in the Genesis Network. It enables new hubs and affiliate mini-hubs to achieve energy/food independence in **days to weeks** and cash-positive status in **1–3 months** by delivering nearly mature, pre-seeded, pre-stocked pods, feedstock, biological starters, and infrastructure directly from mature **staging/mother hubs** or partnered commercial greenhouses.

Without pre-supply, a new hub would require 3–12 months to reach meaningful output. With pre-supply, the timeline collapses by **70–90%**, turning the network into an exponential replication engine.

## Core Components of Pre-Supply Staging

### 1. Staging / Mother Hubs
Any mature Genesis hub (typically Year 1–2: ≥95% closure, $400M–$1B/year revenue, 80–95% lights-out) automatically becomes a **staging/mother hub**.  
- Dedicates **10–20%** of output to supply new sites  
- Produces and pre-seeds pods, feedstock, biological starters, and kits  
- Maintains continuous inventory of near-mature stock (pregnant livestock, established beehives, microbial cultures, dwarf trees)  

### 2. Commercial Greenhouse Multipliers (Level 0.5 Hubs)
Distressed (bankrupt/abandoned) or partnered commercial greenhouses serve as instant staging nodes:  
- Provide massive growing volume immediately  
- Pre-grow mature seedlings, fish fingerlings, BSFL colonies, microbial inoculants  
- Act as forward-operating bases for pod delivery and biological ramp-up  
- Transition to full hubs or remain as dedicated supply nodes  

### 3. Pre-Supply Package (Delivered to New Hub/Affiliate)
Transported via autonomous trailers, trucks, or rail from nearest staging/mother hub or greenhouse:

#### Pods (Pre-Seeded / Pre-Loaded)
- **Ceramic Tile & Pipe Production Pod** — kilns ready, stocked with slag/residues  
- **Aquaponics Pod** — fingerlings, prawns, BSFL, pre-germinated greens  
- **Biogas Digester Pod** — pre-inoculated (stable methane in 1–2 weeks)  
- **Livestock Pod** — near-mature/pregnant animals (staggered cycles)  
- **Beehive Pod** — established hives (10K+ bees, producing within weeks)  
- **Orchard Pod** — dwarf trees (3–6 years old, budding/fruiting)  
- **Processing Pods** — bakery, brewery, butcher, dairy, confectionary (starters loaded)  
- **Microbial Inoculant Pod** — fermenters + cultures  
- **Smart Infrastructure Pod** — pipes/road tiles for instant utility/bee links  

#### Feedstock & Biological Starters
- 50–200 tons starter feedstock (C&D slag, organic wastes, digestate)  
- Microbial inoculant totes (mycorrhizae, nitrogen-fixers)  
- Dwarf tree pots (fabric, root-pruned, inoculated)  
- Pre-sprouted staples trays, fish fingerlings, BSFL colonies  
- Bee starter colonies (established)  
- Livestock breeding pairs + initial feed  

#### Interconnects & Security
- Smart ceramic pipes/tubes (utilities + screened bee transit)  
- Vertical stacking frames (3–10 stories) + horizontal connectors  
- **Smart Airlock Kit** — mmWave/multispectral scanning for weapons/explosives/pathogens

- **Staging Network Density**: Minimum 1 staging/mother hub per 100–200 km radius for efficient delivery  
- **Logistics**: Autonomous trailers + rail extensions (see **[Smart Infrastructure Kit](components/kits-catalog.md)**)

- **Affiliate Integration**: Mini-hubs receive scaled-down pre-supply packages (1–3 pods + starters)
