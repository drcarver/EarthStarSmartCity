# ISO 37123 – Sustainable Cities and Communities  
**Resilience and Smartness Maturity Model**

**ISO 37123:2019** provides a maturity model and indicators to assess city **resilience** (ability to withstand/absorb shocks) and **smartness** (data-driven performance improvement). It complements **ISO 37120** by focusing on adaptive capacity.

Genesis Network targets **Level 4 (Quantitatively Managed)** by 2035 and **Level 5 (Optimizing)** by 2045–2050.

## Maturity Levels

1. **Initial** — Ad hoc response  
2. **Managed** — Basic plans exist  
3. **Defined** — Standardized processes  
4. **Quantitatively Managed** — KPIs tracked & predictive  
5. **Optimizing** — Continuous improvement via data/AI

## Implementation Timeline

- **2027–2030**: Level 2–3 (baseline plans, airlocks, sensors)  
- **2031–2035**: Level 4 (quantified KPIs, predictive analytics)  
- **2036+**: Level 5 (AI-optimized, continuous adaptation)
