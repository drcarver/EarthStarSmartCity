## Objective

ISO/TC 268 is the International Organization for Standardization's Technical Committee for Sustainable Cities and Communities, providing standards for smart, resilient urban development (e.g., ISO 37101 for management systems, ISO 37120 for indicators). In the Genesis Network, **ISO/TC 268** integration ensures hubs and cities meet global benchmarks for sustainability, interoperability, and performance, facilitating regulatory compliance, international licensing, and premium credits.

This enhances viability by +5–10% (faster permitting, higher credits $150–$250/tCO₂e equivalent), with recomputed financials reflecting compliance revenue ($50–$150M/hub/year from audits/contracts).

## Key Integration Strategies

- **Management Systems (ISO 37101)**: Adopt for regenerative planning, using **[digital twins](docs/digital-twins.md)** for continuous optimization of **[closed-loop systems](docs/closure-systems.md)** (energy/water/nutrients).
- **Indicators & Metrics (ISO 37120)**: Embed in **[dense sensor nets](docs/sensors/dense-sensor-net.md)** for real-time KPI tracking (e.g., sequestration via **[Biochar & Soil Amendment Kit](components/kits/biochar-soil-amendment-kit.md)**, water sovereignty via **[AWG & Condensate Recovery](components/systems/awg-condensate-recovery.md)**).
- **Resilience (ISO 37123)**: Align with distributed microgrids (**[Energy Systems LLC](innovation-hub/energy-systems-llc.md)**) and 90+ day isolation; smart airlocks in all buildings for biosecurity.
- **Smart Infrastructure (ISO 37122)**: **[Permeable Road / Path Tile](components/tiles/permeable-road-path-tile.md)** and **[Basic Networking Snap Tile](components/tiles/basic-networking-snap-tile.md)** support IoT/Matter/Thread interoperability.
- **Circular Economy (ISO 37104)**: Waste-derived ceramics in all **[tiles](components/tiles/tile-catalog.md)** and **[pods](components/pods/pod-catalog.md)**; **[Infrastructure Recycling Kit](components/kits/infrastructure-recycling-kit.md)** for full loop.
