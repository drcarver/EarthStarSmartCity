# ISO 37106 – Sustainable Cities and Communities  
**Guidance on Establishing Smart City Operating Models**  
**Genesis Network Implementation Reference**

**ISO 37106:2018** (updated 2022) provides guidance on establishing **smart city operating models** for sustainable, resilient, and citizen-centric urban systems. It emphasizes integrated governance, data-driven decision-making, and technology-enabled services while aligning with **ISO 37101** (sustainable development) and **ISO 37123** (resilience indicators). The standard outlines maturity progression from basic digitization to fully adaptive, AI-optimized cities.

The Genesis Network fully adopts **ISO 37106** as the blueprint for evolving regenerative hubs into smart, self-optimizing cities, leveraging **[pod-centric architecture](../components/pods-catalog.md)**, **[data moat](../docs/data-moat.md)**, and **[telepresence](../components/telepresence.md)** systems.

## 1. Core Principles of ISO 37106

- **Holistic Operating Model**: Integration of people, processes, technology, and governance  
- **Citizen-Centric Focus**: Services designed around resident needs and feedback  
- **Data-Driven Governance**: Open data platforms, KPIs, and continuous improvement  
- **Interoperability & Scalability**: Standards-based systems for seamless expansion  
- **Sustainability & Resilience**: Alignment with UN SDGs and **ISO 37123** resilience  

Genesis targets **Level 4–5 maturity** (advanced/optimized) by 2040s, using **[digital twins](../roadmaps/digital-twins.md)** for real-time simulation and **[AI-driven governance](../strategy/ai-governance.md)** loops.

## 3. Genesis-Specific Smart City Enhancements

- **[Telepresence](../components/telepresence.md)** & VR Decision-Making: Remote governance loops reduce travel; holographic interfaces (post-2040) for immersive planning.  
- **[AI-Driven Governance](../strategy/ai-governance.md)**: Edge AI optimizes resource allocation; predictive models from **[data moat](../docs/data-moat.md)** prevent shortages.  
- **[Pod Mobility](../components/pods-catalog.md)**: Dynamic reconfiguration for disaster response or urban evolution.
