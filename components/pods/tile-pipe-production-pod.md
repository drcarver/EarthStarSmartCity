## Objective

The **Ceramic Tile Production Pod** is the foundational manufacturing unit for producing all **tiles** in the Genesis Network, enabling rapid, waste-derived ceramic production for structures, infrastructure, and kits. It supports the all-electric primary energy model, using **[Energy Storage & Thermal Tile](../tiles/energy-storage-thermal-tile.md)** for power and **[Biogas Digester Pod](biogas-digester-pod.md)** methane as backup. The pod achieves lights-out automation (85–95% mature yield) and integrates with **[Smart Airlock Module Tile](../tiles/smart-airlock-module-tile.md)** for secure access.

## Key Functions & Synergies

- **Tile Production**: Molds, embeds, and fires waste-derived toughened ceramics for all tile types (e.g., **[Roof Glazing (Semi-Transparent BIPV)](../tiles/roof-glazing-bipv.md)**, **[Permeable Road / Path Tile](../tiles/permeable-road-path-tile.md)**).
- **Pipe/Module Fabrication**: Produces pipes and blind-mate interconnects for pods/kits.
- **Synergies**: Inputs from **[Infrastructure Recycling Kit](../kits/infrastructure-recycling-kit.md)** (slag/residues); outputs to all construction (e.g., **[Food Desert Housing Starter Kit](../kits/food-desert-housing-starter-kit.md)**); power from all-electric microgrids via **[Sustainable Energy LLC](../../innovation-hub/sustainable-energy-llc.md)**.

## Manufacturing Process

- **Location**: Mobile pod → permanent factory in productive core zones.
- **Inputs**: 70–95% waste-derived (C&D slag, rice husk ash silica, farm residues, brownfield/ELFM slag from **[Mining & Recycling LLC](../../innovation-hub/mining-recycling-llc.md)**).
- **Materials**: Toughened ceramic composites (60–100+ MPa); embeds (sensors, piezo, PV) from **[TerraFab Manufacturing LLC](../../innovation-hub/terrafab-manufacturing-llc.md)**.
- **Process**:
  1. Feedstock crushing/mixing (waste inputs).
  2. Injection molding/extrusion for shapes.
  3. Embed integration (sensors/antennas).
  4. Firing (1,200–1,400°C, all-electric kilns with biogas backup).
  5. QC via AI/vision + **[Dense Sensor Net Kit](../kits/dense-sensor-net-kit.md)**.
- **Automation**: Lights-out with **[Robotics & Automation LLC](../../innovation-hub/robotics-automation-llc.md)**; yield 85–95%.
- **Standards**: Aligns with **[ISO 14001](../../docs/standards/iso-14001.md)** (sustainable) and **[ISO/TC 268](docs/standards/iso-tc-268.md)** (embedded smart features).

## Recomputed Costs & Revenues (per Pod, February 2026 Baseline)

- **CAPEX**: $200–$400k (down 5–10% via all-electric kilns; phased 2027–2030).
- **OPEX**: 15–25% of revenue (energy from microgrids reduces costs 10–15%).
- **Production Cost**: $16–$140/m² depending on tile type (waste inputs lower 15–20%).
