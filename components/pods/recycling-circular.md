# Recycling & Circular Pod  
**Genesis Network Component Specification**

The **Recycling & Circular Pod** is a core module in the Genesis Network responsible for processing waste streams into high-value feedstocks and materials, achieving near-100% material closure within hubs. It serves as the primary engine for the **circular economy** pillar, converting demolition debris, brownfield slag, farm residues, ELFM outputs, and municipal waste into inputs for **[toughened ceramic tiles](../components/tiles-catalog.md)**, **sustainable concrete bases**, and other products.

## 1. Pod Overview & Purpose

- **Primary Function**: Accept, sort, crush, vitrify, and refine waste into usable aggregates, slag, syngas, and recovered metals  
- **Role in Network**: Closes material loops; supplies **[tile production pods](../components/tiles-catalog.md)** and **[biorefinery pods](../components/pods-catalog.md)**; generates tipping fees and carbon credits  
- **Capacity (Mature Hub)**: 50–200 tons/day input; 40–180 tons/day output (tiles feedstock, syngas, recovered metals)  
- **Mobility**: Trailer-mounted or pod-relocatable for brownfield/landfill remediation campaigns  

## 2. Manufacturing & Assembly

- **Produced in**: **[Tile Production Pod](../components/tiles-catalog.md)** + assembly line within hub  
- **Shell**: Waste-derived toughened ceramic (95–100% by 2035), with embedded **Faraday mesh** for EMP protection  
- **Key Components**:  
  - Mobile crushers & shredders (ceramic-lined)  
  - Plasma vitrification torch (licensed unit)  
  - Magnetic & eddy-current separators  
  - Syngas capture & cleaning module  
  - Robotic sorting arms (from **[Robotics & Automation Pod](../components/pods-catalog.md)**)  
- **Assembly Time**: 4–8 weeks (pre-supplied modules + on-site ceramic cladding)  

## 3. Input Sources & Materials

| Input Stream                  | Primary Sources                          | % of Total Input | Processing Method                     | Output Products                          |
|-------------------------------|------------------------------------------|------------------|----------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Construction & Demolition (C&D)** | Road contracts, urban demolition         | 40–60%           | Crushing → sorting → plasma            | RCA aggregate, slag for tiles            |
| **ELFM / Landfill Mining**    | Closed landfills, brownfields            | 20–40%           | Excavation → shredding → vitrification | Vitrified slag, recovered metals         |
| **Farm & Organic Residues**   | Crop waste, manure, processing trimmings | 10–20%           | Shredding → syngas fermentation        | Biochar, syngas, nutrient ash            |
| **Municipal Solid Waste (MSW)** | Affiliate tipping fees                   | 5–15%            | Sorting → plasma / gasification        | Syngas, vitrified residue                |

**Input Quality Target**: 70–95% waste-derived (virgin materials <5% after 2030)

|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|-----------------------------------------|----------------------------|---------------------------|-------|
| **Vitrified Slag**            | 30–120                      | Feedstock for **[toughened ceramic tiles](../components/tiles-catalog.md)** | 80–150                     | 300–800                   | Primary tile input |
| **Recycled Concrete Aggregate (RCA)** | 20–80                | Road bases, foundations                 | 15–40                      | 100–300                   | Low-carbon concrete |
| **Recovered Metals**          | 1–5                         | TerraFab feedstock                      | 500–2,000                  | 200–600                   | Ferrous/non-ferrous |
| **Syngas / Biochar**          | 5–20 (syngas equiv.)        | Energy + soil amendment                 | 100–300 (energy equiv.)    | 150–400                   | Carbon credits uplift |
| **Tipping Fees**              | —                           | Waste acceptance                        | 20–80 / ton input          | 400–1,200                 | Affiliate subsidized |

**Total Revenue per Pod (Mature)**: **$1.2–3.3M/year**  
**Gross Margin**: 65–85% (high due to waste inputs + credits)

## 5. Integration & Standards Compliance

- **Inputs from**: Smart roads, **[construction contracts](../strategy/infrastructure-upgrades.md)**, affiliates  
- **Outputs to**: **[Tile Production Pod](../components/tiles-catalog.md)**, **[biorefinery pods](../components/pods-catalog.md)**, **[TerraFab](../components/pods-catalog.md)**  
- **Standards Alignment**:  
  - **[ISO 14001](../docs/standards/iso-14001.md)** – Environmental management  
  - **[ISO 37123](../docs/standards/iso-37123.md)** – Resilience & smartness indicators  
  - **Genesis-STD-004** – Circularity metrics (95%+ closure)  
- **Smart Airlocks**: Dual airlocks with mmWave/multispectral scanning for biosecurity and contraband  

## 6. Viability Enhancements

- **Plasma Vitrification** → Zero landfill residue; slag reused in tiles  
- **Syngas Capture** → Energy + CO₂ for DAC pilots or beverages  
- **Blockchain Credits** → Monetize avoided emissions ($150–250/tCO₂e)  
- **Mobile Deployment** → Rapid brownfield remediation contracts
