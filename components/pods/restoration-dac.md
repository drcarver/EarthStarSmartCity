# Restoration & DAC Pilot Pod

The **Restoration & DAC Pilot Pod** is a modular, 40 ft high-cube ISO container-based unit designed to advance **ecosystem restoration**, **blue-green carbon sequestration**, and **direct air capture (DAC)** within the Genesis Network. It serves as both a field-deployable restoration engine and a test platform for technologies that enhance gigaton-scale carbon negativity and climate resilience.

This pod integrates **blue-green carbon kits**, **microbial inoculant production**, **DAC modules**, and **monitoring arrays** to remediate degraded lands, restore wetlands/rivers, and capture atmospheric CO₂ for mineralization into tiles or aggregates.

## 1. Pod Design & Specifications

- **Base Unit**: 40 ft high-cube ISO container (~300 m² effective volume with vertical racking)  
- **Shell**: Recycled steel (early) → 95–100% **toughened ceramics** (waste-derived, munitions-resistant 8–9/10)  
- **Power**: 5–20 kW (**BIPV glazing** 10–30% + **biogas microturbine** + piezo floors); sodium-ion batteries (2–5 days autonomy)  
- **Water**: 95–99% closure via **AWG**, rainwater, digestate recovery  
- **Airlocks**: Dual **smart airlocks** with mmWave/multispectral scanning (biosecurity, pathogen/weapons detection)  
- **Automation**: Lights-out robotics for planting, monitoring, maintenance; RISC-V edge AI  
- **Mobility**: Autonomous wheeled/tracked platform; amphibious variants for coastal/wetland deployment

## 3. Integration with Genesis Network

- **Feedstock**: Uses **C&D slag**, **ELFM slag**, **farm residues**, and **digestate** from livestock/aquaponics pods  
- **Energy**: Waste heat from **plasma vitrification** and **biogas microturbines** powers DAC  
- **Outputs**:  
  - Sequestered CO₂ mineralized into **toughened ceramics** (tiles, aggregates)  
  - Biodiversity & carbon credits ($150–250/tCO₂e) via blockchain verification  
  - Restored ecosystems feeding **blue-green carbon** loops  
- **Feedback Loops**: Data from pod sensors refines **microbial inoculants** and **DAC efficiency** → improves network-wide sequestration
