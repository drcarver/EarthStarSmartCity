## 1. Overview & Mission

**TerraFab (Electronics & Appliances) LLC** is one of the 19 independent LLCs in the **[Innovation Hub](strategy/innovation-hub-overview.md)**. It is responsible for sovereign, regenerative manufacturing of electronics, appliances, sensors, edge compute, robotics components, and select pharmaceuticals using waste-derived ceramics, 3D printing, and closed-loop processes.

**Mission**  
Internalize high-value manufacturing for the Genesis Network while minimizing virgin mining through recycling, plasma refining, and modular designs. Produce niche sovereign chips (RISC-V edge AI, sensors) and DC-native appliances to enable lights-out automation, digital twins, and distributed intelligence.

**Key Principles**  
- Pod-centric manufacturing (Mini-Fab → advanced fab clusters)  
- 70–95% waste-derived inputs (e-waste, slag, RHA silica)  
- Self-funded via Network revenues; no external investors  
- Strict export controls (sensitive tech remains U.S./NATO-aligned)  

## 2. Core Capabilities & Outputs

- **Semiconductors**: Mature-node (65–130 nm) → advanced (28–3 nm) RISC-V chips for edge AI, sensors, robotics controllers  
- **Appliances**: DC-native (refrigerators, ovens, washers, entertainment systems)  
- **Sensors**: Hyper-dense, self-powered arrays for **[Sensor Handbook](docs/sensors/sensor-handbook.md)**  
- **Robotics Components**: Swarm bot controllers, actuators, vision modules  
- **Pharma/Generics**: Select high-volume generics (tablets, injectables) via ceramic bioreactors

## 4. Input Sources & Materials

- **Primary Inputs**: E-waste (recycled via **[Recycling & Plasma Vitrification LLC](components/recycling-plasma.md)**), slag/RHA silica from **[Ceramic Tile & Infrastructure LLC](components/ceramic-tile-infrastructure.md)**  
- **Materials**: Waste-derived ceramics + reclaimed silicon/REEs; plasma-refined metals  
- **Energy**: Biogas + BIPV from **[Smart Grid & Infrastructure LLC](components/smart-grid-infrastructure.md)**  

## 5. Integration with Network

- **Supplies**: Edge compute for **[Data Moat & Digital Twin LLC](components/data-moat-digital-twin.md)**; sensors for all pods  
- **Receives**: Ceramic shells from **[Ceramic Tile & Infrastructure LLC](components/ceramic-tile-infrastructure.md)**; power from biogas  
- **Risk Isolation**: Independent LLC – delays do not impact food/energy pods

## 7. Viability Enhancements Specific to TerraFab

- **Phased Rollout**: Start mature-node (low risk); use revenues for advanced EUV  
- **Niche Focus**: Sovereign edge AI/sensors avoid TSMC competition
