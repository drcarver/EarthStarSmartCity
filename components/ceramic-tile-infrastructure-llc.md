## 1. Overview & Mission

**Ceramic Tile & Infrastructure LLC** is the foundational LLC within the Genesis Network's 19-LLC innovation hub. It owns and operates all ceramic tile production facilities, smart pipe networks, road systems, and structural kits that form the physical backbone of every **[Genesis Pod](components/genesis-pods.md)**, hub, housing cluster, and regenerative city.

**Mission**  
- Produce 70–95% waste-derived toughened ceramics at scale  
- Embed active smart features (BIPV, AWG, piezo, sensors, Faraday mesh)  
- Enable snap-together, modular construction of all Network buildings  
- Deliver internal zero-cost materials + external high-margin sales  
- Achieve full compliance with smart city and sustainable building standards  

This LLC is the primary revenue engine in early phases (2027–2035), funding self-replication and other LLCs.

## 2. Core Responsibilities

- Design, manufacturing, and continuous improvement of all **[Ceramic Tiles](components/ceramic-tiles.md)** (roof glazing, cladding, structural, permeable, compute/networking)  
- Production of **[Smart Pipes](components/smart-pipes.md)** (bidirectional power/water/waste/data/slurry/bee transit)  
- Development and deployment of **[Flat-Pack Construction Kits](components/construction-kits.md)** for pods, housing, factories, roads  
- Smart road/bridge/rest stop systems (piezo/solar, permeable, heating, sensors)  
- Infrastructure recycling integration (RCA concrete bases + ceramic cladding)  
- Standards leadership: ISO/TC 268 (smart cities), IEEE 2030 (grid), ITU-T Y.4900, ISO 14001, Cradle-to-Cradle  

## 3. Manufacturing Process & Facilities

**Production Path**  
1. **[Tile Production Pods](components/tile-production-pods.md)** (initial bootstrap, 5–10 pods/site)  
2. Temporary kilns/tents (immediate output)  
3. Permanent cluster retrofits (6–12 months, 5–10× throughput)  
4. Lights-out automation (70–95% by 2035)  

**Inputs (70–95% Waste-Derived)**  
- C&D slag, rice husk ash silica, farm residues (bagasse, pomace), ELFM slag, brownfield materials  
- Minor virgin additives early → phased out  

**Outputs**  
- All Network tiles/pipes (internal zero-cost)  
- External sales (premium smart tiles)  
- Surplus for affiliates/smallholders

## 5. Smart Airlocks & Security Integration

All structures built by this LLC include **mandatory dual smart airlocks**:  
- mmWave + hyperspectral scanning for weapons, explosives, pathogens  
- Instant alert to on-site security (robotic + human)  
- Integration with **[Sensor Handbook](docs/sensor-handbook.md)** for anomaly detection  
- Compliance: MIL-STD-810 ruggedness, IEEE 2030 security
