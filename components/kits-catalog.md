# Genesis Network Kits Catalog

**Version Date:** January 2026  
**Prepared by:** EarthStar Technologies Conceptual Framework  
**License:** This work is licensed under a [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

All kits are flat-pack, containerized, self-replicating systems delivered via mature **Tile Production Pods** or permanent factories. They enable rapid hub/affiliate deployment, replication, and scaling. Every kit is designed for on-site assembly using only **toughened ceramic tiles**, pipes, and waste-derived materials produced locally.

**Manufacturing Process**  
- Produced in **Tile Production Pods** (bootstrap) → permanent cylindrical factories (retrofit 6–12 months).  
- Inputs: 70–95% waste (C&D slag, rice husk ash silica, farm residues, ELFM/brownfield slag); minimal virgin materials early.  
- Automation: 50–70% lights-out Day 1 → 80–95% mature.  
- All kits include assembly instructions, digital twins, and edge AI for telepresence oversight.
