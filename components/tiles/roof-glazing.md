# Roof Glazing (Semi-Transparent)  
**Genesis Network Tile Specification**

**Executive Overview**  
The semi-transparent roof glazing tile is a high-ROI, multi-functional component designed for greenhouse roofs, community housing, and other light-permeable structures. It combines 10–30% light transmittance for plant growth with embedded thin-film BIPV for power generation, integrated AWG condensers for water harvest, and dense sensor arrays for real-time environmental monitoring. Manufactured from 80%+ waste-derived materials (primarily rice husk ash silica), it achieves rapid payback through electricity, water, and premium produce revenue while supporting 95–99% closure in mature hubs.

**Viability & Integration Notes**  
Roof glazing delivers the fastest ROI of all tiles (power + daylighting + water + premium produce). It forms the backbone of greenhouse pods and community roofing, enabling early cash flow while supporting 95–99% closure. Integration with microbial inoculants and dense sensors creates compounding yield gains. Primary risk is initial PV embed precision – mitigated by parallel testing in tile pods.
