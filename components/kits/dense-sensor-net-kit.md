## Objective

The **Dense Sensor Net Kit** deploys a modular, hyper-dense sensor network across Genesis hubs, affiliates, brownfields, landfills, and future off-world sites. It provides real-time environmental, structural, biosecurity, and occupancy data to feed **[digital twins](docs/digital-twins.md)** for predictive maintenance, compliance, and optimization.

Key capabilities:
- Ground-based radar for mapping and perimeter security
- Starlink nodes for rural internet hotspots (free in Genesis zones/TerraWell)
- Integrated sensors for reclamation tracking on landfills/brownfields (compliance credits)
- Extensible to Luna, Mars, and other planets/moons for monitoring and resource mapping

The kit supports **[smart airlocks](components/kits/smart-airlock-kit.md)** in all buildings (mmWave/hyperspectral + trace detectors for weapons/explosives; silent alerts to security). It aligns with smart city standards (**[ISO/TC 268](docs/standards/iso-tc-268-smart-cities.md)**, IEEE 2030, ITU-T Y.4900, Matter/Thread) and sustainable building codes (**[ISO 14001](docs/standards/iso-14001-environmental.md)**, Cradle-to-Cradle).

## Manufacturing & Deployment

- **Process**: Assembled in **[Tile & Pipe Production Pods](components/pods/tile-pipe-production-pod.md)** → permanent factories. Sensor embeds during ceramic molding; final QC with AI/vision.
- **Inputs**: 70–95% waste-derived (C&D slag, rice husk ash silica, farm residues, brownfield/ELFM slag); electronics from **[TerraFab Manufacturing LLC](innovation-hub/terrafab-manufacturing-llc.md)**.
- **Materials**: Toughened ceramics (60–100+ MPa) for housings; low-power, recyclable sensors.
- **Deployment Density**:
  - 1 sensor per 10–50 m² in productive/residential zones
  - Radar/Starlink nodes at perimeters, gates, and high-traffic areas
  - Plug-and-play with **[Basic Networking Snap Tile](components/tiles/basic-networking-snap-tile.md)**
- **Mandatory vs. Optional**: Required in all hubs for compliance; optional (on request) for affiliates to minimize regulatory burden.
- **Smart Airlock Integration**: All buildings include **[Smart Airlock Module Tile](components/tiles/smart-airlock-module-tile.md)** connected to the net; sensors detect threats and trigger alerts via **[Basic Security & Monitoring Tile](components/tiles/basic-security-monitoring-tile.md)**.
