# Biogas Digester & Upgrading Kit

The Biogas Digester & Upgrading Kit is a self-replicating, modular system for converting organic waste into renewable biogas (methane-rich) and nutrient-rich digestate fertilizer, serving as a cornerstone for energy independence and soil regeneration in the Genesis Network. Core products include anaerobic digesters, gas upgrading modules, and microturbines for power generation, with services focused on waste-to-energy integration. Timeline: Pilots in 2027–2028, mature scaling by 2030–2035. Revenue role: Generates $4–15B/year network-wide (mature 2040s) through energy exports, tipping fees, and digestate sales, with 60–80% margins.

## Viability & Integration Notes

The Biogas Digester & Upgrading Kit is highly viable (8.5/10) due to its central role in energy self-sufficiency and waste closure, with low CAPEX and rapid ROI (12–24 months). Integrates seamlessly with waste valorization and sustainable energy LLCs; enhances sequestration credits via digestate. Timeline: Pilots 2027–2028, full maturity 2030–2035. Financials recalibrated for 70–95% waste inputs, yielding 60–80% margins and $4–15B/year network revenue (2040s).
