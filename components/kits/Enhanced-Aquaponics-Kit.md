# Enhanced Aquaponics Kit

**Description**: Modular flat-pack kit enabling rapid deployment of **multi-trophic aquaponics** systems in a 40 ft high-cube pod or cluster configuration. Integrates fish, crustaceans, mollusks, insects, and plants into a closed-loop ecosystem achieving **95–99% resource closure**, continuous staggered production, and high-value edible outputs (greens, premium seafood, insect protein).

This kit is a core component of the Genesis Network's **pod-centric architecture**, designed for bootstrap acceleration, affiliate mini-hubs, and resilience in diverse climates (freshwater dominant, with saltwater pilot variants).

## Kit Contents & Variants

| Variant                            | Primary Species                          | Target Outputs                          | Capacity (per pod) | Use Case |
|------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|---------------------|----------|
| **Standard Freshwater**            | Tilapia + prawns + BSFL + mussels + greens | Greens, fish, prawns, insect feed       | 5–15 t edible protein/year | General hubs & affiliates |
| **Premium Protein**                | Catfish/Trout + crayfish + mussels + herbs | High-value fish, crayfish, herbs        | 8–20 t edible protein/year | Cold-climate & specialty markets |

| **Waste Conversion Focus**         | BSFL/mealworms dominant + fish scraps    | Insect protein (feed & human-grade)     | 2–8 t insect biomass/year | Livestock/processing integration |

## Manufacturing & Assembly

- **Primary Production**: **Tile Factory Pods** (kilns, extruders, plasma vitrification) produce ceramic tanks, trays, pipes, and structural elements  
- **Assembly**: **Robotics & Automation Pods** + manual/edge AI guidance for bio-seeding (fingerlings, juveniles, BSFL colonies, microbial inoculants)  
- **Pre-Supply**: Delivered pre-seeded from **staging/mother hubs** or partnered greenhouses → first harvest Week 3–6
