# Earth-Star Industries – National Transformation Project  
**A closed-loop, waste-to-civilisation megaproject (2035–2095)**  
*One supply chain. One material system. One nation rebuilt.*

[![CC BY 4.0](https://i.creativecommons.org/l/by/4.0/88x31.png)](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

## What This Is
A complete, regulator-ready, bankable blueprint to transform the continental United States using **only** waste-derived ceramic tiles and flat-pack kits produced by the Genesis Facility network.

- 7,200 × MHUE-50k smart cities (360 million people housed & fed)  
- 9,300 × hybrid Skyscraper Farms (100 % replacement of conventional agriculture)  
- 720 million acres rewilded  
- 82,000 miles of 220 mph high-speed rail on existing freight corridors  
- Full disaster resilience up to and including nuclear war and nuclear winter  
- Permanent self-funding achieved 2051 — zero public money required after that date

All documents are written to November 2025 technical, legal, and financial reality — **no speculative materials, no impossible timelines, no physics violations**.

## Core Principles (non-negotiable)
1. **Ceramic tiles are 100 % non-structural** – cladding only, exactly like terra-cotta rainscreen or glass curtain wall  
2. All technology exists at commercial or paid-pilot scale in November 2025  
3. Permanent self-funding by 2051 – public credits are a 15-year booster only  
4. Every city and farm is energy-positive, water-positive, and food-secure  
5. Full resilience to nuclear war, super-volcano, or 1 km asteroid
