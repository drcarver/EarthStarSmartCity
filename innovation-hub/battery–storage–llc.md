## Objective

The **Battery & Storage LLC** develops and manufactures sodium-ion batteries using waste-derived materials, integrated into distributed energy storage tiles and pods for DC microgrids. This LLC supports the Network's energy independence, enabling 90+ day autonomy in hubs, cities, affiliates, and off-world extensions. Batteries tie into **BIPV glazing** ([components/tiles/roof-glazing-semi-transparent-bipv.md](components/tiles/roof-glazing-semi-transparent-bipv.md)), **waste heat recovery** ([docs/energy-water-closure-systems.md](docs/energy-water-closure-systems.md)), and **smart infrastructure pods** ([components/pods/smart-infrastructure-pod.md](components/pods/smart-infrastructure-pod.md)) for resilient power storage.

Key features: Waste anodes (e.g., hard carbon from farm residues), modular hot-swappable tiles, self-powered via piezo/BIPV, and federation with digital twins for predictive maintenance. Phase 1 (2026–2030) focuses on mature-node integration; advanced nodes deferred to contingencies ([strategy/advanced-fab-contingency-strategy.md](strategy/advanced-fab-contingency-strategy.md)).

## Core Components

- **Sodium-Ion Cells**: Waste-derived anodes/cathodes; 100–300 Wh/kg; 5,000+ cycles; safe (non-flammable).
- **Storage Tiles**: Snap-in ceramic casings with phase-change thermal buffering; 100–300 kWh/cluster.
- **Pods**: Larger banks for hub-scale storage (1–10 MWh); integrate with **biogas digester pods** ([components/pods/biogas-digester-pod.md](components/pods/biogas-digester-pod.md)).
- **DC Microgrids**: Low-voltage distribution tied to **energy systems LLC** ([innovation-hub/energy-systems-llc.md](innovation-hub/energy-systems-llc.md)).

## Manufacturing & Materials

- **Process**: Tile factories produce ceramic casings; **Mini-Fab** ([innovation-hub/advanced-fab-llc.md](innovation-hub/advanced-fab-llc.md)) assembles cells; blind-mate integration.
- **Input Sources**: Waste anodes (farm residues, biochar); cathodes from recycled sodium (brine processing via **water purification LLC** ([innovation-hub/water-purification-desalination-llc.md](innovation-hub/water-purification-desalination-llc.md))).
- **Materials**: 70–95% waste-derived ceramics + sodium-ion chemistries; compliant with **sustainable building codes** ([docs/standards/sustainable-building-codes.md](docs/standards/sustainable-building-codes.md)).
- **Prod Cost**: Tiles $60–$140/m²; Pods $150–$350k/unit (mature, down 15–20% via waste efficiencies).
- **Selling Price**: Tiles $200–$400/m²; Pods $300–$700k/unit; margins 60–80%.

## Suggestions to Enhance Viability (Implemented)

- **Waste Synergies**: 100% anodes from farm residues/biochar; reduces costs 15–25%; +10% viability.
- **Affiliate Bundling**: Storage kits with **energy storage starter kit** ([components/kits/energy-storage-starter-kit.md](components/kits/energy-storage-starter-kit.md)); +20% adoption.
- **Pre-Supply Expansion**: Stockpile cells in bootstrap; reduces bottlenecks 20–30%.
- **Dual-Sourcing**: Anodes from multiple waste streams; +15% resilience.
- **Off-World Adaptation**: Radiation-hardened variants for Luna/Mars; +10% long-term revenue ($50–$200B/year 2060+).
