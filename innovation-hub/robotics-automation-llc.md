## Sensors Handbook

**Purpose & Standards**  
**[Sensors](docs/sensors/sensors.md)** form the data moat: 3–5× redundancy in every **[tile](components/tiles/ceramic-tiles.md)** and **[pod](components/pods/general-pods.md)** for ruthless optimization. Comply with **[IEEE 1451](docs/standards/ieee-1451.md)** (transducers), **[OGC SWE](docs/standards/ogc-swe.md)** (sensor web), **[ISO 14001](docs/standards/sustainable-building-codes.md)** (environmental), **[MIL-STD-810](docs/standards/mil-std-810.md)** (ruggedness).

| Category                 | Types & Functions                                                                       | Redundancy | Integration | Calibration / Lifespan | Cost per Unit ($) | Network Revenue Uplift ($B/year, mature) |
|--------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|-------------|------------------------|-------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| **[Environmental Sensors](docs/sensors/environmental-sensors.md)** | Temp, humidity, PAR, VOC, CO₂, ammonia (climate/stress monitoring)                      | 3–5×       | All tiles/pods | Annual / 10–15 years   | 5–20              | 2–8                                       |
| **[Structural Sensors](docs/sensors/structural-sensors.md)** | Strain, vibration, tilt (maintenance/seismic)                                           | 3–5×       | Load-bearing tiles | 15–25 years            | 8–25              | 1–5                                       |
| **[Power Sensors](docs/sensors/power-sensors.md)** | Voltage, current, piezo output (energy optimization)                                    | 4×         | BIPV/piezo tiles  | 10–20 years            | 10–30             | 3–10                                      |
| **[Water Sensors](docs/sensors/water-sensors.md)** | Flow, pH, EC, turbidity (closure/safety)                                                | 3–5×       | AWG/plumbing tiles | 8–12 years             | 15–40             | 2–7                                       |
| **[Security Sensors](docs/sensors/security-sensors.md)** | mmWave radar, hyperspectral (weapons/explosives/pathogens)                              | 3–5×       | Airlocks/perimeters | 10–15 years            | 20–60             | Indirect (risk reduction)                 |
| **[Biological Sensors](docs/sensors/biological-sensors.md)** | Microbial activity, nutrients, root density (yield prediction)                          | 3×         | Aquaponics/agroforestry | 5–10 years             | 12–35             | 4–15                                      |
| **[Off-World Sensors](docs/sensors/off-world-sensors.md)** | Radiation, regolith composition, seismic (ISRU/habitat integrity)                       | 4–6×       | Lunar/Mars pods   | 20–30 years            | 50–150            | 5–20                                      |

**Smart Airlocks**: All buildings feature **[smart airlocks](docs/standards/smart-airlocks.md)** scanning for weapons/explosives/pathogens (mmWave + hyperspectral) → alert security AI.
