# Cultured Meat LLC

The Cultured Meat LLC is a deferred-phase entity in the Genesis Network Innovation Hub, focused on scalable, regenerative production of lab-grown meat using multi-trophic bioreactors, stem cell lines, and nutrient recycling from hub wastes. It leverages closed-loop systems to achieve 95–99% resource closure, producing high-quality proteins (beef, poultry, seafood equivalents) at costs 30–50% below traditional farming by 2040. Phase 4 rollout (2035+); estimated CAPEX $120–350M per hub; revenue $200–600M/year mature (premium sales + exports); IRR 35–55%.
