# Recycling & Plasma Vitrification LLC

The **Recycling & Plasma Vitrification LLC** is one of the 19 core entities in the Genesis Network **Innovation Hub**. It focuses on transforming waste streams (C&D debris, ELFM slag, brownfield materials, municipal solid waste fractions) into high-value feedstocks for the Network's **ceramic tile** production, while generating syngas for energy and recovering metals.

## Mission & Role

- **Primary Mission**: Achieve near-100% waste valorization for circular economy closure  
- **Network Integration**: Supplies vitrified slag/aggregates to **Tile Production Pods** and factories; syngas to **Biogas** systems; metals to **TerraFab**  
- **Revenue Model**: Tipping fees (subsidized for affiliates), syngas sales, metal recovery, premium aggregates  
- **Viability Impact**: Reduces virgin mining dependency; generates early revenue (tipping fees); enables gigaton-scale sequestration via waste avoidance  

## Technologies & Processes

- **Mobile Plasma Vitrification Units**: High-temperature plasma torches (3,000–10,000°C) melt waste into inert vitrified slag  
- **Feedstock Preparation**: Mobile crushers + sorters separate recyclables (metals, combustibles)  
- **Syngas Capture**: Plasma gasification produces syngas (CO + H₂) for microturbines  
- **Metal Recovery**: Electromagnetic separation + slag leaching for precious/rare metals  
- **Automation**: 80–95% lights-out (robotic handling, sensor-driven process control)  

## Input Sources & Materials

- **Primary Inputs**: C&D waste (concrete, asphalt, brick), ELFM slag, brownfield soils, farm residues, MSW fractions  
- **Secondary Inputs**: Digestate solids, processing byproducts  
- **Material Output**:  
  - Vitrified slag (70–90% of input) → ceramic feedstock  
  - Syngas → energy  
  - Metals (steel, aluminum, copper, REEs) → **TerraFab**
