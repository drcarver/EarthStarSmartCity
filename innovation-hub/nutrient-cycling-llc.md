## Objective

**Nutrient Cycling LLC** is responsible for achieving near-complete nutrient closure across the Genesis Network by converting organic wastes, digestate, and CO₂ into high-value soil amendments, microbial inoculants, biochar, and mineralized carbon products. The LLC operates as a risk-isolated entity within the 32-LLC innovation hub structure, focusing on:

- Converting biogas digestate and farm residues into premium inoculants and biochar  
- Implementing enhanced weathering and mineralization for permanent carbon sequestration  
- Supplying closed-loop fertilizers to **[Regenerative Hubs LLC](innovation-hub/regenerative-hubs-llc.md)**, **[Vertical Urban Farming LLC](innovation-hub/vertical-urban-farming-llc.md)**, and affiliates  
- Generating revenue through premium soil products, sequestration credits, and affiliate kits  

All processes use waste-derived inputs (70–95%), modular ceramic equipment from **[Tile & Pipe Production Pod](components/pods/tile-pipe-production-pod.md)**, and dense sensor nets for optimization/compliance.

## Core Functions & Processes

1. **Digestate Processing**  
   - Input: Anaerobic digestate from **[Biogas Digester Pod](components/pods/biogas-digester-pod.md)**  
   - Output: Liquid nutrient concentrate + solid fraction for biochar/inoculants  

2. **Biochar Production**  
   - Pyrolysis of residues (crop stalks, manure solids, food waste) in ceramic kilns  
   - Co-benefits: Waste heat recovery to **[Energy Systems LLC](innovation-hub/energy-systems-llc.md)**  

3. **Microbial Inoculant Cultivation**  
   - Fermentation of tailored consortia (mycorrhizal fungi, nitrogen-fixers, PGPR) using digestate feedstock  
   - Parallel testing in **[Microbial Inoculant Pod](components/pods/microbial-inoculant-pod.md)** variants  

4. **Enhanced Weathering & Mineralization**  
   - Spread basalt dust + biochar on fields; captured CO₂ mineralization into aggregates/tiles  
   - Integration with **[Restoration Kits LLC](innovation-hub/restoration-kits-llc.md)** for blue/green carbon  

5. **Closed-Loop Distribution**  
   - Internal supply to hubs/affiliates; external premium sales + credit generation  

## Integration with Other LLCs

- **[Biogas Digester System](innovation-hub/energy-systems-llc.md)**: Primary digestate supplier  
- **[Regenerative Hubs LLC](innovation-hub/regenerative-hubs-llc.md)** & **[Vertical Urban Farming LLC](innovation-hub/vertical-urban-farming-llc.md)**: Main consumers of inoculants/biochar  
- **[Restoration Kits LLC](innovation-hub/restoration-kits-llc.md)**: Joint blue/green carbon projects  
- **[Tile & Pipe Production Pod](components/pods/tile-pipe-production-pod.md)**: Supplies ceramic kilns/fermenters  
- **[Energy Systems LLC](innovation-hub/energy-systems-llc.md)**: Waste heat symbiosis  

## Manufacturing & Materials

- **Equipment**: Ceramic pyrolysis kilns, fermenters, spreaders from tile factories  
- **Inputs**: 70–95% waste-derived (digestate, residues, basalt dust from brownfields)  
- **Process**: Lights-out automation (85–95% mature); AI-optimized via data moat (parallel testing for strain/yield refinement)
