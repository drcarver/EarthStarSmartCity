## Objective

The **Hydrogen Production LLC** produces green hydrogen (H₂) from surplus renewable power in Genesis hubs, integrating with [**biogas digester pods**](components/pods/biogas-digester-pod.md) for baseload stability. It supports fuel cells for [**autonomous mobility**](innovation-hub/autonomous-mobility-llc.md), industrial processes, and export, while enabling hydrogen bunkering for [**maritime & ports**](innovation-hub/maritime-ports-llc.md). Region-specific adaptations accommodate cultural/religious needs (e.g., halal-certified processing in relevant areas).

## Core Features

- **Electrolyzer Modules**: PEM or alkaline stacks using surplus [**BIPV**](components/tiles/roof-glazing-semi-transparent-bipv.md) and biogas power.
- **Integration**: Co-located with [**biogas digester system**](components/pods/biogas-digester-pod.md) for stable input; output stored in ceramic tanks or fed to [**high-speed rail**](innovation-hub/high-speed-rail-transport-llc.md).
- **Output**: 100–500 kg H₂/day per hub (mature); purity 99.999% for fuel cells.
- **Co-Benefits**: Oxygen byproduct for [**industrial gases**](innovation-hub/industrial-gases-llc.md); waste heat recovery for [**greenhouses**](components/pods/aquaponics-pod.md).

## Manufacturing & Materials

- **Process**: Assembled in [**TerraFab**](innovation-hub/terrafab-manufacturing-llc.md) using [**ceramic tiles**](components/tiles/ceramic-tiles.md); electrolyzer stacks from mini-fab (28–7 nm control chips).
- **Input Sources**: Surplus power (BIPV/biogas); water from [**AWG recovery**](docs/standards/sustainable-building-codes.md).
- **Materials**: Waste-derived ceramics (70–95%: C&D slag, RHA silica); platinum/iridium catalysts minimized via AI optimization.
- **Prod Cost (mature $/kg H₂)**: $1.5–$3 (down 10–15% via waste efficiencies); module cost $100–$250k/unit.

- **CAPEX**: $100–$250M (electrolyzers + storage; offset 20–30% by CHIPS grants for clean energy).

- **Revenue Streams**:
  - H₂ sales (mobility/industrial): $150–$400M/year (margins 60–80%).
  - Oxygen byproduct: $50–$100M/year.
  - Credits/contracts (IRA 45V $3/kg H₂): $100–$200M/year.
- **Total Revenue**: $300–$700M/year; Net +$180–$420M/year.
- **Network-Wide (2050)**: $50–$100B/year (tiles/kits only 40–50% of total).
- **Payback**: 2–4 years; IRR 30–45%.
