## Executive Overview

TerraFab LLC is the dedicated entity for sustainable, regenerative manufacturing of electronics, appliances, sensors, edge compute hardware, and related components within the Genesis Network. Operating as a deferred high-margin LLC (post-2030 triggers), TerraFab focuses on mature-node semiconductors (65–130 nm initial, scaling to advanced nodes), RISC-V-based SoCs, DC-native appliances, and high-value niche chips for internal Network use (.AI/robotics/digital twins) and selective external leasing. Revenue role: $400M–$900M/hub/year mature (60–85% margins); network-wide $40–150B/year by 2050. Phase: Deferred (2030–2033 Mini-Fab launch; 2035+ advanced). Self-funded via Earth revenue flywheel; critical for sovereign compute and appliance internalization.

## Viability & Integration Notes

TerraFab provides sovereign compute critical for Network autonomy (AI, twins, robotics) while generating high-margin revenue to accelerate self-funding. Risk isolated via modular Innovation Hub structure; dependencies on ceramics/recycling mitigated by on-site production. Deferred phase protects early cash flow; data moat from Earth hubs drives yields 20–60% above competitors.
