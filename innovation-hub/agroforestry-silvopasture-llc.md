# Agroforestry & Silvopasture LLC  
**Specification Sheet**

## Executive Overview

The Agroforestry & Silvopasture LLC integrates multi-layer tree crops, perennial polycultures, and rotational grazing systems across low-density productive zones to maximize long-term carbon sequestration, biodiversity, soil regeneration, and diversified high-value outputs (fruits, nuts, timber, fiber, forage). Operating within Phase 2–3 (2028–2040 rollout, mature by 2040s), the LLC delivers premium regenerative products while enhancing overall hub closure and resilience. Revenue role: +$300–$600M/year per mature hub from premium fruits/nuts/fiber, forage sales, and sequestration credits; critical for MyPlate diversity and long-term carbon-negative performance.

## Viability & Integration Notes

The Agroforestry & Silvopasture LLC is a high-ROI, low-risk enhancer: leverages existing livestock/orchard pods for rapid deployment, delivers front-loaded premium revenues, and provides long-term sequestration/moat data. Integration with microbial inoculants and restoration kits creates compounding yields (+20–40% over time). Political appeal strong (rural jobs, biodiversity); minimal new bottlenecks.
