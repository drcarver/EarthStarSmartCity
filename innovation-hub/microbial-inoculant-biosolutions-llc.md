## 1. Overview & Mission

The **Microbial Inoculant & Biosolutions LLC** is one of the 19 core LLCs in the Genesis Network Innovation Hub. Its mission is to produce customized microbial inoculants and biosolutions that boost soil health, plant yields, and ecosystem resilience across all Network hubs, affiliate farms, and restoration projects.

**Key Objectives**  
- Achieve **10–35% yield increases** in **aquaponics pods**, **agroforestry**, and **restoration sites** through tailored microbial consortia  
- Convert waste streams (digestate, plant trimmings) into high-value inoculants  
- Enable rapid soil regeneration on brownfields/landfills  
- Provide scalable kits for smallholder farmers globally  
- Maintain **95–99% closure** in microbial production loops  

This LLC operates in dedicated **Microbial Inoculant Production Pods** and integrates with **Biorefinery & Processing Pods** and **Restoration & Blue-Green Carbon** initiatives.

## 3. Manufacturing & Supply Chain

**Production Location**: Dedicated **Microbial Inoculant Production Pods** (40 ft high-cube ceramic shells) co-located with **Biorefinery Pods**. All pods/kits sourced from **Tile Production Facilities**.  

**Inputs**  
- Feedstock: Digestate from biogas digesters, plant trimmings, fish sludge  
- Cultures: Initial strains from mature hubs (pre-seeded in IBC totes)  
- Nutrients: Waste-derived carbon/nitrogen sources  

**Materials & Composition**  
- Pod shell: Toughened ceramic (waste-derived)  
- Fermenters/vessels: Ceramic-lined stainless or full ceramic  
- Sensors: Integrated into pod tiles (pH, DO, temperature, VOC)  

**Manufacturing Path**  
- Seed cultures from staging hubs  
- Fermentation in pod bioreactors (batch/continuous)  
- Drying/encapsulation (freeze-drying or spray-drying)  
- Packaging into flat-pack kits  

**Cost & Revenue**  
- Production Cost per Kit: $0.4–$1.0M  
- Selling Price per Kit: $1.0–$2.5M  
- Annual Revenue (mature Network): $1–$4B (kit sales + internal use credits)  
- Margins: 65–85% (automation + waste-derived inputs)
