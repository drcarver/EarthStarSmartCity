## Objective

**Sustainable Energy LLC** is the dedicated entity responsible for achieving and maintaining **energy sovereignty** across all Genesis hubs, affiliates, regenerative cities, and future off-world extensions. It integrates multi-source renewables, waste heat recovery, distributed storage, and smart microgrids to deliver:

- **95–100% energy independence** within 6–18 months of hub maturity
- **Surplus export** for revenue and affiliate subsidies
- **Resilience** against grid failures, disasters, and blackouts (90+ day autonomy)
- **Carbon-negative operations** via waste-derived inputs and sequestration synergies

The LLC operates in parallel with other hub entities (e.g., **[Biogas Digester System](components/pods/biogas-digester-pod.md)**, **[Energy Storage Tiles](components/tiles/energy-storage-tile.md)**) while owning advanced integrations and export strategies.

## Key Technologies & Inputs

- **Primary Inputs** (70–95% waste-derived): Farm residues (biogas), C&D slag (ceramic conduits), brownfield materials (storage casings).
- **Manufacturing**: All components from **[Tile & Pipe Production Pod](components/pods/tile-pipe-production-pod.md)** → permanent factories.
- **Standards Compliance**: IEEE 2030 (smart grid), ISO 14001 (sustainable), ITU-T Y.4900 (smart city energy).
