# Mining & Recycling LLC

**Objective**  
The **Mining & Recycling LLC** focuses on sustainable, lights-out mining for critical materials (REEs, lithium, cobalt where needed) and aggressive recycling of infrastructure/e-waste via plasma torches/ELFM to minimize virgin extraction. It integrates with the Network's circular economy, feeding slag/metals into **Tile & Pipe Production Pod** and ceramics production. Operations are region-specific (e.g., co-located near existing mines in U.S./Canada; recycling-focused in Europe). Cultural/religious accommodations: Ethical sourcing audits, community co-ops in mining areas.

## Core Operations

- **Sustainable Mining**: AI/robotics for precision, low-impact extraction; plasma for clean ore processing (smelting/recovery → ingots + slag + syngas).
- **Recycling**: Mobile plasma torches for autos/ships/buildings/roads/e-waste; black mass recovery for batteries; sediments/sludge from dams/ports → nutrients/biochar.
- **Integration**: Slag to **Tile & Pipe Production Pod**; metals to **TerraFab Manufacturing LLC**; syngas to **Biogas Digester Pod**. Brownfield/landfill focus ties to **Restoration Kits LLC**.
- **Automation**: Lights-out (AI/drones/autonomous harvesters); waste heat to **Waste Heat Recovery System**.
