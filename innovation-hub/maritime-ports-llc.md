# Maritime & Ports Infrastructure LLC

## Executive Overview

The Maritime & Ports Infrastructure LLC extends the Genesis Network's regenerative principles to coastal and port environments, focusing on upgrading docks, terminals, and vessels with ceramic cladding for corrosion resistance, drag reduction, and embedded smart features. It incorporates hydrogen bunkering for clean fuel, waste processing for circular economy integration, and resilient infrastructure for disaster-prone areas. This LLC operates in Phase 3 (2030–2035) of the Genesis Network rollout, with initial CAPEX of $120–280M per hub and projected annual revenue of $250–700M from port contracts, cladding sales, and bunkering fees.
