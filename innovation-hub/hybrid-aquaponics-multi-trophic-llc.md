# Hybrid Aquaponics & Multi-Trophic LLC

**Executive Overview**  
The Hybrid Aquaponics & Multi-Trophic LLC operates scalable, closed-loop production systems integrating fish, crustaceans, mollusks, insects (BSFL), and plants to deliver high-value proteins, greens, and premium seafood while achieving 95–99% water and nutrient closure. Core products include fresh tilapia/catfish, prawns/crayfish, mussels, BSFL protein feed, and MyPlate-compliant greens/herbs/staples. Systems are deployed in dedicated pods and support hub food security, retail supply, and affiliate kits. Rollout begins 2026–2027 (pilot pods); full maturity 2030–2035. Revenue role: primary early cash driver (40–60% of hub food revenue), mature contribution $80–250M per hub/year (high margins from premium multi-trophic outputs).
