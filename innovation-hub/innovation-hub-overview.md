## Prioritization Logic

LLCs are ordered by **earliest viable implementation** and **revenue/cash-flow impact** to accelerate self-funding:

1. **Immediate bootstrap & high-margin early revenue** (tiles, food, biogas)  
2. **Core closure & waste valorization** (processing, energy recovery)  
3. **Yield & sequestration boosters** (microbials, restoration)  
4. **Manufacturing & electronics** (TerraFab, robotics)  
5. **High-value diversification** (pharma, gases, mobility)  
6. **Global & humanitarian enablers** (smallholder support, TerraWell)
