# MHUE-50k Smart City Complex: Executive Summary and Project Plan (First Commercial Node – 2035)

## Executive Summary

The MHUE-50k is a fully integrated, financially self-sustaining 50,000-resident smart city built exclusively with Genesis Facility ceramic flat-pack kits on a 3,200 ha brownfield site co-located with a Genesis waste-processing factory, Skyscraper Farm, and TerraFab node (West Virginia flagship).  
All non-structural cladding, façades, pavers, and interior finishes are 100 % waste-derived ceramic tiles. Primary structure remains conventional IBC-compliant steel/concrete.  
The city is a net exporter of electricity (+380 MW), potable water (+11 Mm³/yr humid climate), and verified carbon credits while providing luxury-level housing (classified as low-income for tax/zoning purposes), free city services, and high-wage jobs.

First node groundbreaking: 2 January 2035. Full occupancy: Q4 2041.  
Total CAPEX: $28.7 billion (2035 USD). Break-even: 2044. NPV (2035–2050, 6 %): $84.3 billion. IRR: 34 %.  
Nationwide rollout: 8 nodes by 2045 → 80 nodes by 2060.
