## 1. Pod Overview & Strategic Role

**Genesis Aquaponics Pods** (40 ft high-cube ISO containers, ~76 m³ base volume → ~300 m² effective with vertical racking) are the scalable, mobile core for multi-trophic protein + produce production. Enhancements boost yields 15–30%, closure 95–99%, and revenue via crustaceans/mollusks/insects integration. Freshwater dominant (80–90%); saltwater pilots coastal-only. Pods drive 20–40% of hub revenue, accelerate self-funding (Month 6–12), and enable exponential replication.

**Recomputed viability impact**: +10–20% network IRR (to 35–55%); faster bootstrap (timelines shortened 2–4 months via pre-seeding + multi-trophic efficiency).

## 2. Base Specifications (Updated for 2026 Projections)

- **Structure**: Steel-to-ceramic transition (95–100% waste-derived by 2032); BIPV glazing roof (10–30% transmittance, 100–300 Wp/m²)
- **Power/Water**: 5–25 kW (BIPV + biogas); 10–60 kWh sodium-ion storage; 150–500 L/day AWG + recovery (95–99% recirculation)
- **Automation/Biosecurity**: Lights-out robotics; hyper-dense sensors (3–5× redundancy); dual airlocks with mmWave scanning
- **Mobility**: Autonomous/amphibious variants
- **Setup Timeline**: 1–3 months (pre-seeded); full production 2–4 months post-deployment
- **Per-Pod Cost (2026 est.)**: $75k–$250k (up 20–30% from 2022 baselines due to enhancements; offset by 15–25% yield gains)
- **Annual Yield/Revenue**: 1–5 tons protein + 10–30 tons produce; $350k–$950k (fish 50–60%, premiums 20–30%, insects/feed 10–20%)

## 3. Multi-Trophic Layers & Synergies

| Layer              | Species (Freshwater Primary / Saltwater Pilot) | Density/Cycle                  | Role & Yields (per pod/year)   |
|--------------------|------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **Fish**           | Tilapia/Catfish/Trout / Barramundi/Red Drum    | 500–5,000; 6–12 mo staggered  | Dominant protein; 0.5–2.5 tons |
| **Crustaceans**    | Prawns/Crayfish / Shrimp variants              | 5–15/m²; 3–6 mo staggered     | Waste cleanup + edible; 0.2–0.8 tons |
| **Mollusks**       | Mussels/Clams / Oysters (pilot)                | High in beds; continuous       | Filtration; 0.1–0.5 tons (optional edible) |
| **Insects**        | BSFL/Mealworms/Crickets                        | Weekly harvest bins            | Waste-to-feed; 0.3–1 ton (feed/protein) |
| **Plants**         | Greens/Herbs/Staples / Salicornia/Kelp         | 8–12 cycles (greens); 3–6 (staples) | Uptake + output; 10–35 tons   |

**Synergies**: Waste → insects/crustaceans → filtration (mollusks) → plants; +10–30% yields via inoculants; data moat optimizes (50–500 parallel tests).

## 4. Pod Variants

- **Standard**: Tilapia + prawns + BSFL + greens ($350k–$750k revenue)
- **Premium**: Catfish/Trout + crayfish + mussels + herbs ($450k–$950k)
- **Saltwater Pilot**: Barramundi + kelp + BSFL ($500k–$1.2M; coastal only)
- **Waste-Focused**: BSFL/mealworm dominant ($150k–$450k feed value)
- **Cluster**: 5–15 linked pods for full multi-trophic scale
