# Earth-Star “Open Door” Global Licensing Programme (Part 6A)  
**First non-Western-Hemisphere / non-NATO district under Open Door:** 2 January 2081  
**Trigger condition:** Fortress West Phase 3 complete (≥1,800 districts operational & revenue-positive) + Earth-Star board vote

## Core Mission
After Fortress West is irreversibly successful and Earth-Star holds an uncopyable 50-year lead in waste-ceramic systems, the company opens the exact same licensing package offered to NATO + Western Hemisphere partners to any sovereign nation on Earth — on identical, non-negotiable terms.

This is not charity. It is the controlled, profitable, defensively-smart way to prevent the technology from ever being reverse-engineered by hostile actors while still capturing 4–6 % of gross construction value worldwide for the next 150+ years.

## Aggressive but 100 % Honest Feasibility Statement

This is the only way a Western company can safely globalise a transformative infrastructure technology in the 21st–22nd century without losing control or being copied into irrelevance.

- The 50-year U.S. lead (2035–2085) is unassailable  
- The proprietary moat (stealth, data centers, batteries, airlocks) remains absolute  
- The 5 % royalty is politically acceptable because it is cheaper than any alternative construction method after 2060  
- The NC/SA clause + factory JV + quality gate prevents instant Chinese commoditisation  
- Excluded states are permanently locked out unless regime change occurs

Result: Earth-Star becomes the de facto infrastructure backbone of the entire non-hostile world while remaining a private, highly profitable corporation in perpetuity.
