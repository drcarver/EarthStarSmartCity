# TerraWell FreeCare System: Executive Summary and Master Plan (Prompt 03A)

## Executive Summary

The TerraWell FreeCare System is a permanently 100 % free nationwide (later global) humanitarian network operated by the independent 501(c)(3) TerraWell Foundation. It provides unlimited food, generic/OTC medicines, medical devices, urgent care, primary care, mental health services, and mobile disaster response to every citizen with no co-pays, no means testing, and no government ID required.

The entire program is funded exclusively by fair-market-value (FMV) donations from Earth-Star Industries (for-profit entities) to the nonprofit Foundation. These donations are fully deductible under IRC §170(b)(1)(A) at full retail value, creating a tax shield that renders the program cash-flow neutral to Earth-Star by ~2041 and cash-positive thereafter.

First 24 fixed centers + 80 mobile units operational Q1 2035.  
By 2050: 1,200 fixed centers + 2,000 mobile units serving 120 million Americans annually at zero charge.  
Cumulative FMV donated 2035–2075: >$8 trillion → cumulative federal tax savings >$2.4 trillion (30 % blended rate).  
Net cost to Earth-Star after tax shield (2035–2075): effectively zero after 2042, then permanent multi-billion-dollar annual profit center.

All facilities are built exclusively with Genesis Facility ceramic flat-pack kits on brownfield land adjacent to (but legally separate from) Genesis waste-processing sites. Complete legal, accounting, and data firewalls are maintained at all times.

## 1. Detailed Legal & Tax Structure (Text Description)

Structure is a classic private foundation + operating charity hybrid with maximum IRS compliance:

- TerraWell Foundation (Delaware 501(c)(3) public charity, EIN issued 2034)  
  – Independent 9-member board (no Earth-Star employees or family members >20 % overlap)  
  – Separate campus, separate EMR, separate payroll, separate cloud tenant  
  – Receives only in-kind FMV donations (never cash from Earth-Star after initial seed)  

- Donation flow (all at documented FMV):  
  Earth-Star Industries LLC → donates finished goods & services → TerraWell Foundation → distributes free to public  

- Valuation method:  
  – Food: USDA Thrifty Food Plan × 1.35 markup (IRS-accepted 2025 precedent)  
  – Drugs & devices: Average Wholesale Price (AWP) minus 12 % (Red Book standard)  
  – Clinical services: Medicare Allowable + 15 % (IRS Rev. Rul. 69-545)  

- Annual independent appraisal by Big-4 firm + Form 8283 non-cash charitable contributions filed by Earth-Star.

## 6. Integration Rules with Core Earth-Star Industrial Stack

Integration is strictly prohibited by design:

- No shared facilities, no shared driveways, no co-branding on same site  
- No patient data ever flows to for-profit entities (HIPAA + separate EMR)  
- No Earth-Star employees may serve on TerraWell board for >5 years after employment  
- All TerraWell centers located minimum 500 m from any Genesis/Farm/TerraFab fence line  
- Separate utility meters, separate Internet circuits, separate security teams  
- Donated goods are physically transferred at FMV with bill of lading and third-party inspection

The only allowed interaction: one-way physical donation of finished goods from Earth-Star loading dock to TerraWell receiving dock.
