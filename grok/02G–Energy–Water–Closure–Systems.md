## Objective

Deliver **near-complete energy, water, and nutrient independence** for every hub, mini-hub, and regenerative city within 6–18 months of launch — while generating early surplus revenue to self-fund expansion.

**Core constraint**: Limit to **6 essential closure systems** for rapid deployment, low complexity, and fast cash flow.

## Core Systems (All Implemented)

1. **BIPV Glazing & Roof Tiles**  
   - Semi-transparent solar glazing + full BIPV cladding  
   - Output: 80–220 Wp/m² (roofs) + 8–22 W/m² (walls)  
   - Co-benefits: Daylight, thermal mass, AWG condensate capture  
   - Revenue: Surplus power export + energy credits  

2. **Biogas Digester System**  
   - Pre-inoculated digesters + microturbines  
   - Input: Manure, food waste, green cuttings  
   - Output: Methane → electricity/heat + digestate → fertilizer/inoculants  
   - Revenue: Tipping fees + power sales + fertilizer credits  

3. **AWG & Condensate Recovery**  
   - Atmospheric water generators + tile-embedded microchannels  
   - Output: 1,200–2,800 gallons/hub/year (humid climates)  
   - Co-benefits: Cooling + humidity recapture  
   - Revenue: Excess water recharge/export + drought-prone affiliate sales  

4. **Sodium-Ion Storage Tiles & Pods**  
   - Distributed sodium-ion banks + phase-change thermal buffering  
   - Capacity: 100–300 kWh per hub cluster  
   - Co-benefits: Grid stabilization, night-time power  
   - Revenue: Microgrid leasing + peak shaving contracts  

5. **Waste Heat Recovery System**  
   - Thermoelectric generators + microchannel exchangers in tile/pod design  
   - Sources: Kilns, biogas engines, biorefinery  
   - Output: 100–300 GWh/year recoverable heat/electricity  
   - Revenue: Symbiosis partnerships + efficiency credits  

6. **Nutrient & Carbon Cycling Loop**  
   - Digestate → microbial inoculants → biochar → soil amendment  
   - CO₂ → enhanced weathering/biochar sequestration  
   - Revenue: Carbon/biodiversity credits + premium soil products

## Viability & Strategic Notes

This streamlined closure suite eliminates 60–70% of original energy/water complexity while preserving **full independence** and **early cash flow**. Biogas tipping + BIPV surplus + sequestration credits provide the fastest ROI. The system is politically neutral (energy/water security appeals across ideologies) and funds the broader network (tiles, housing, TerraWell) without external capital.

**Stretch Goals (Deferred)**: DAC pilots, hydrogen production, advanced thermal batteries — only after core loop is proven profitable at scale.
