## 1. Objective & Design Constraint

Deliver **near-complete energy, water, and nutrient independence** for every hub, mini-hub, and regenerative city within **6–18 months** of launch — while generating early surplus revenue to self-fund broader network expansion (tiles, housing, TerraWell, affiliates).

**Core Constraint**: Limited to **exactly 6 essential closure systems** — proven, revenue-positive, low-complexity technologies that fund the rest of the network. Advanced/exotic elements (DAC, hydrogen, etc.) deferred to stretch goals after core loop profitability.
