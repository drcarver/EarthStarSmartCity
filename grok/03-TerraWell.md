# TerraWell FreeCare System  
**Consolidated Executive Specification & Financial Integration**  
(Standalone 501(c)(3) Humanitarian Layer – December 2025 – January 2026)

## Executive Summary

**TerraWell FreeCare System** is an independent 501(c)(3) nonprofit operated by the TerraWell Foundation, providing 100% free medical care, nutritious food, over-the-counter & select generic medications, durable medical equipment (DME), and competency-based education/training to underserved populations with no means testing, co-pays, or identification required.

**Mission**: Deliver immediate humanitarian relief and long-term empowerment through free health services, essential goods, and regenerative skill-building aligned with UN SDGs.

**Scale Goals (2050)**:  
- 500–1,000 fixed care & training centers  
- 2,000–5,000 mobile response & distribution units  
- Annual reach: 50–100 million individuals  
- Annual FMV goods distributed: $10–20 billion  
- Trained individuals (competency modules): 10–20 million cumulative  
- Indirect carbon-negative contribution: support for gigaton-scale rewilding via trained affiliates

**Net Financial Impact to EarthStar Technologies (for-profit parent)**:  
- Annual §170(e)(3) enhanced inventory deductions: $1–3 billion (primarily food + qualifying medical supplies)  
- Corporate tax savings: $250–750 million/year (at 21–25% effective rate)  
- Reputational & political viability uplift: +10–15% to overall Genesis Network project success probability  
- Talent pipeline: 10–20M trained individuals → potential affiliate workforce & community goodwill  
- No direct cash outlay from EarthStar; funding via FMV donation of surplus inventory only

## Legal & Tax Structure

- **Entity**: TerraWell Foundation – independent 501(c)(3) public charity  
- **Governance**: Separate board (no EarthStar overlap), independent bylaws, annual Form 990 audits  
- **Funding Mechanism**: Solely FMV donations of tangible inventory from EarthStar Technologies  
  - Eligible for enhanced §170(e)(3) deduction (up to 2× cost basis for food & certain medical items for the ill/needy)  
  - Standard §170 deduction for non-qualifying goods  
  - No cash, services, IP, credits, or personnel transfers permitted  
- **Regulatory Compliance**: FDA (generics via ANDA, OTC), HIPAA (patient data), IRS (no private inurement)  
- **Firewall**: No shared data systems, personnel, facilities, or marketing; arm’s-length leasing of brownfield sites only
