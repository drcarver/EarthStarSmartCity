# Earth-Star Skyscraper Farm – Commercial Node #1  
**First commercial groundbreaking:** 2 January 2032  
**Location:** Central Valley, California (2,100 ha brownfield + adjacent farmland acquisition)  
**Target population fed:** 20,000 people (USDA MyPlate diversity + halal/kosher/vegan options)

## Executive Summary
The Skyscraper Farm is a hybrid vertical + horizontal regenerative food-energy-water campus built 100 % with flat-pack ceramic kits from the Genesis Facility (Prompt 1).  
A 150 m tall, 15-floor vertical CEA tower produces all leafy greens, berries, mushrooms, shrimp, tilapia, cultured meat, bakery goods, and cleanroom nutraceuticals.  
A contiguous 2,065 hectare (5,100 acre) horizontal regenerative zone — fully covered by Genesis ceramic greenhouses, agrivoltaic canopies, and plug-in windmill kits — grows calorie-dense crops, orchards, nuts, grains, cotton, and raises cattle, sheep, pigs, and poultry under 2025-proven no-till / silvopasture systems.

**Key validated outcomes (2035 steady-state):**
- Land efficiency: 12.4× vs 2025 U.S. average
- Net electricity export: 185 GWh/yr
- Net water export (humid-season equivalent sites): 180 million litres/yr
- Annual revenue: $1.84 billion (food 72 %, cultured meat 12 %, nutraceuticals 9 %, carbon credits 4 %, energy 3 %)
- Total development cost: $2.76 billion (fully kit-based)
- Break-even: Year 5 (2036)
- NPV (2032–2050, 6 %): $9.41 billion
- IRR: 28 %
