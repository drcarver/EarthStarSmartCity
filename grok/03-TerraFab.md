**Project start date (first commercial node):** 2 January 2031 (post-Genesis online; co-located with Genesis Facility and Skyscraper Farm)

## Core Mission
Construct and operate a nationwide network of co-located TerraFab facilities: advanced, sustainable semiconductor manufacturing nodes integrated with Genesis ceramic production and Skyscraper Farm outputs. Each TerraFab shall:  
- Produce 3 nm-class AI chips (e.g., logic for NVIDIA/AMD equivalents) at 30,000 wafers per month (WSPM) steady-state, using hybrid ceramic/virgin materials where proven.  
- Maximize 3D-printed components (4–6% of fab mass; e.g., tooling, ducts, brackets) via Genesis 3D printers and farm-derived bio-resins.  
- Manufacture sustainable medical supplies (e.g., non-sterile devices, sensors) and generic/OTC drugs (tablets/capsules only) in ≤5% dedicated space, leveraging farm APIs and ceramic kits.  
- Feed compliance portal with sensor data from all equipment kits for real-time NASA/DOD/ISO/FDA tracking.  
- Achieve 70% below 2025 global fab emissions via biogas from farm waste + RECs; 90–92% water recycling.  
- Fund phases via Genesis/Farm revenue (35–40%) + CHIPS Act grants/loans (55–65%); enable rewilding by reducing farmland chemical needs through on-site drug APIs.  
- Do as much permitting and construction in parallel as possible.  
- 3D print as much as possible using Genesis printers; evaluate co-location with Genesis/Farm for synergies.  
- Add farm crops (e.g., yeast for vitamins, algae for binders) to support drug production.

## Mandatory Technical Constraints (validated November 2025)

1. **Fab Structure**: Conventional steel/concrete primary frame (IBC-compliant) with Genesis ceramic non-structural cladding (rainscreen, heat-recovery panels). Cleanrooms: ISO 1–7 via conventional stainless/modular panels; Genesis ceramics for walls/support only.  
2. **Process Node**: 3 nm logic (ASML EUV, Applied Materials/TSMC-licensed tools); no sub-2 nm until 2038+. Capacity: 30,000 WSPM (300 mm wafers) in 2036 steady-state.  
3. **3D Printing Scope**: 4–6% mass (Lithoz/Nanoe/XJet tech): ceramic tooling, metal brackets, spares. No EUV/pressure vessels; outsource virgin high-purity parts. Integrate farm bio-materials for non-critical resins.  
4. **Equipment Kits**: Flat-pack for support (racks, gas cabinets) with embedded sensors (IoT for compliance). Critical tools (EUV scanners, implanters) ship factory-integrated (ASML/TEL standard; CHIPS IP-compliant). TerraBot™-assembled where possible.  
5. **Medical/Drug Integration**: ≤5% floorspace for non-sterile generics/OTC (e.g., ibuprofen tablets via farm-extracted APIs) and devices (sensors, gauze from ceramic/bio-fibers). Steriles/biologics in separate FDA facility post-2040. Add farm crops: yeast (vitamins), herbs (extracts), algae (binders).  
6. **Energy**: Biogas from farm/Genesis waste (20 MW CHP, 62% efficiency); grid RECs for Scope 2. Scope 3: 650 kt CO₂e/year (silane/chemicals); target 70% below global avg via supplier audits.  
7. **Water**: 90–92% recycling (Gradiant CFRO + RO); AWG from ceramic panels; zero net in humid WV.  
8. **Emissions/Security**: Near-zero Scope 1+2; DoD 5000.89 EMP shielding (interior copper/ceramic); smart airlocks at entrances.  
9. **Co-Location**: Adjacent to Genesis (ceramics/feedstock) + Farm (biomass/APIs); shared utilities/grid.  
10. **Expansion**: Phases funded by prior revenue; replicate 1/node/year post-2036.
