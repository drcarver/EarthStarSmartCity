## Project Overview

The Genesis Communicator Badge is a compact, durable wearable that bridges personal use with Network infrastructure, offering voice commands, biosensing (HR/HRV/SpO₂/temperature/fall detection), and precise positioning. It prioritizes privacy (local processing, opt-in federation) and resilience (self-powered via piezo/BIPV/solid-state battery). Bundled WiFi tiles connect to fiber backbones, providing plug-in public WiFi hotspots free in Genesis zones/TerraWell centers to boost accessibility and adoption.

## Deployment Strategy

- **Manufacturing**: Ceramic injection molding in tile factories; blind-mate pockets for modules; final firing/QC.
- **Distribution**: Free in TerraWell centers (trial); discounted subscription ($5–$15/month) to keep; bundled with WiFi tiles for affiliates/residents.
- **WiFi Tiles**: Plug-in to infrastructure/fiber; free public WiFi in Genesis; enhance badge connectivity.
- **Affiliate/Retail**: Variants for general/health/pro markets; upsell to tile purchases/Network affiliation.
- **Off-World**: Radiation-hardened variant for tracking/navigation.

## Key Performance Characteristics

- **Physical**: 40–55 mm × 10–14 mm; 22–38 g; IP67; MIL-STD-810G.
- **Compute**: 4–8 core RISC-V (28–7 nm); 2–8 GB RAM; on-device LLM (Phi-2/TinyLlama).
- **Sensors**: PPG (HR/HRV/SpO₂); IMU (motion/fall); thermopile (temperature); microphone array.
- **Positioning**: RTK GNSS (1–3 cm in-network); cellular/GPS fallback (5–20 cm global).
- **Networking**: mmWave/inductive to tiles (primary); cellular/BLE fallback.
- **Audio**: Bone-conduction + directional mic.
- **Power**: Piezo + micro-BIPV + 80–150 mAh solid-state; 7–14 days standby (tile mesh).
- **Security**: Open-source seL4 microkernel; local-first; E2E encryption.
- **WiFi Tiles**: Plug-in hotspots; fiber backbone connect; free in Genesis/TerraWell.
