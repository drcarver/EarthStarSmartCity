# Genesis Network – 50-Year Space Roadmap with NASA/ISS Tech Feedback Loops

**EarthStar Technologies** | Version: February 2026 | Prepared for Stakeholders, Partners, and Potential Affiliates

The Genesis Network's 50-year roadmap (2026–2076) integrates Earth-based regenerative hubs with space expansion (Luna, Mars, asteroids, O'Neill cylinders, Von Neumann probes). NASA/ISS-derived tech (e.g., closed-loop life support, hydroponics, radiation shielding from ISS modules like Bigelow Expandable Activity Module, ECLSS systems) is explicitly marked. Feedback loops: Space experiments → data to Earth digital twins/moat for optimization → improved tech deployed to lunar/Mars. Sensor nets aid planning/simulations. Financials recalculated with space uplift (+20–40% revenues from materials/habitats; CAPEX +$200–600B 2040–2070).
