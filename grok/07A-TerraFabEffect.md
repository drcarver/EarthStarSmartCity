## Why the Effect Is So Large

1. **Revenue density** — A single 3 nm fab in 2040 prices generates ~$480 M/yr pure profit on ~80 ha (same land as one big-box store).
2. **Compounding flywheel** — That profit funds 4–6 new cities per year instead of 2 → true exponential growth.
3. **Sanction-proofing** — No city ever again depends on TSMC/Samsung/Intel for leading-edge chips.
4. **Geopolitical leverage** — Countries beg to join Fortress West / Global phase because they get sovereign 3 nm → 1 nm capacity.
5. **Public funding becomes irrelevant** — After the first 20–30 cities with TerraFab™ are online (≈2044), the project is permanently self-financing at 6–8× the original speed.

## Remaining Risks (Still Exist)

- Qualification timeline — Real 46–54 months from groundbreaking to first 3 nm wafer (ASML High-NA delay risk).
- Talent — Need ~2,800 PhD-level process engineers per fab (mitigated by on-site K-16 + equity packages).
- Export control — USG could try to block High-NA EUV export (mitigated by domestic ASML partnership + black-budget co-funding).
