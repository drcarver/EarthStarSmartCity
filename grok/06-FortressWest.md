## 10. Aggressive but 100 % Honest Feasibility Analysis (November 2025)

**Why This Is Geopolitically Credible**  
- Starts only after overwhelming U.S. domestic proof (>50 cities profitable)  
- Uses NC/SA license that explicitly blocks Chinese/Russian commoditisation  
- Keeps every true strategic technology (stealth, AI, batteries) 100 % proprietary  
- Funding stack mirrors proven 2020–2025 EU Green Deal / IDB model, not fantasy money  
- HSR limited to politically viable corridors only

**Hard Constraints Honoured**  
- No NATO money for civilian cities  
- No pure open-source structural tiles  
- No export before 2045  
- No forced eminent domain outside existing ROW

**Remaining Risks & Mitigations**  
- Currency risk → mitigated by 60/40 JV and USD-denominated royalties  
- Political flip-flopping → contractual 50-year royalty lock-in + liquidated damages  
- Local corruption → Earth-Star retains tile certification authority (no cert = no subsidies)

**Verdict:**  
Fortress West is the first and only hemispheric-scale infrastructure programme that is simultaneously profitable for Earth-Star, defensively aligned with NATO, financially self-sustaining after 2057, and fully realisable with nothing beyond 2025 technology, law, and financing instruments.
