# Earth-Star Genesis Facility – Executive Summary & Bankable Project Plan  
**West Virginia, USA | Project Start: 2 January 2027 | First Revenue: May 2027 | Full Capacity: Q1 2030**

The Genesis Facility is the world’s first full-scale closed-loop waste-to-ceramic-cladding megafactory. It accepts gate fees to remediate brownfields, closed/open landfills, C&D debris, ELVs, ship scrap, and industrial byproducts—including raw sewage sludge—while producing only high-value non-structural ceramic panels, rainscreen systems, pavers, and smart building components from 100 % recycled feedstock.

All ceramic products are strictly non-structural cladding/infill (identical in function to terracotta rainscreen or glass curtain wall). Primary structure remains conventional steel or recycled-content concrete, fully IBC/ASCE 7 compliant.

The facility bootstraps inside a leased high-performance tent campus (2027–2029), generating revenue and profit for 30 months before a single permanent building exists. The permanent campus is then erected exclusively using proven flat-pack kits assembled by TerraBot™ cobots.
