## Detailed Breakdown of Existential-Level Threats

### 1. 1-km Asteroid (Tunguska × 1,000)
- Direct hit on city: 0 % survival.
- 99.9 % of strikes miss city → multi-year winter survivable with 6-year grain + vertical farm stack.
- Rating reflects global effects only.

### 2. Supervolcano (Yellowstone VEI-8)
- Ash loading collapses conventional roofs → ceramic towers rated for 3 m ash + acid rain resistance.
- 10–15 years sunlight block → full indoor CEA proven at 1/10th natural light.
- Bottleneck: vitamin D production (solved with on-site UVB LEDs + cultured supplements).

### 3. Full-Scale Nuclear Exchange
- City deliberately sited >800 km from ICBM fields, command centers, and top-20 metro targets.
- Fallout: 1,000 rad/hr outside → 15-year sealed operation possible with ceramic shielding + positive pressure.
- Nuclear winter: 12–15 °C cooling → indoor farms run 24/7 on biogas + geothermal.
- EMP: DC native grid + ceramic Faraday panels → lights never flicker.

### 4. Engineered Pandemic
- Hospital has 500-bed negative-pressure isolation wing built with TerraMed™ ceramic walls.
- On-site mRNA/lentiviral manufacturing (2025 tech) → vaccines in <60 days.
- City can seal for 3 years without external supply.
