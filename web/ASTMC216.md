## ASTM C216 Compliance Details

**Overview**: ASTM C216 covers solid masonry units (clay/shale-based) for structural/facing use, classified by durability (Grades: SW – Severe Weathering for high freeze-thaw; MW – Moderate Weathering) and appearance (Types: FBS – general; FBX – precise tolerances; FBA – architectural variation). Requirements include compressive strength (min 3,000–12,500 psi by grade/type), absorption (max 8–20% by grade), dimensional tolerances (±1/16–3/16 in.), and efflorescence resistance.

**Genesis Tile Alignment** (Meets/Exceeds as Toughened Ceramics Analog):  
- **Grades**: All tiles qualify for SW (severe weathering) via enhanced formulations (waste slag/RHA boosts freeze-thaw resistance >100 cycles; low absorption <5% via vitrification).  
- **Types**: Structural tiles as FBS/FBX (tight tolerances ±1/32 in. via robotic molding); cladding/patterned as FBA (cultural designs for aesthetic variation).  
- **Physical Properties**: Compressive strength 8,000–15,000 psi (exceeds min); water absorption <4% (below max); no efflorescence (glaze/sealants).  
- **Dimensional/Chippage**: Length/width/height tolerances ±1/16 in.; chippage limits (≤1/2 in. per ASTM) met via quality AI inspection.  
- **Testing**: Per ASTM C67 (sampling/testing); Genesis adds embedded strain sensors for real-time compliance monitoring.  
- **Enhancements**: Waste-derived composition (70–95%) exceeds standard natural clay/shale; embedded conduits/sensors add smart functionality without compromising specs.

## ASTM C1405 Compliance Details

**Overview**: ASTM C1405 covers single-fired glazed brick with fused ceramic glaze, for masonry use. Classified by exposure (Class: Exterior/Interior), selectivity (Grade: S – select), type (I/II based on imperfections), and void area (Division: Solid ≤25% voids; H40V 25–40%; H60V 40–60%). Requirements: Compressive strength (min 3,000–6,000 psi), absorption (max 6–10%), glaze durability (imperviousness, chemical resistance), crazing/opacity tests.

**Genesis Tile Alignment** (Meets/Exceeds):  
- **Classes/Grades/Types**: All as Exterior, Grade S, Type I (minimal imperfections) via high-temp fusion (1,200–1,400°C).  
- **Divisions**: Structural as Solid (<10% voids); permeable/insulating as H40V/H60V (controlled porosity for AWG/drainage).  
- **Physical Properties**: Compressive 60–100+ MPa (~8,700–14,500 psi, exceeds min); absorption <3% (below max); glaze impervious (no penetration per acid/alkali tests).  
- **Glaze Durability**: No crazing (thermal shock resistance >200°C differential); opacity/chemical resistance per ASTM C650/C1370 (exceeds via vitrified waste matrix).  
- **Testing**: Per ASTM C67/C650 (strength/absorption/chemical); Genesis integrates hyperspectral sensors for ongoing glaze integrity.  
- **Enhancements**: Single-fired with embedded BIPV/AWG (glaze-compatible); waste-derived (RHA silica for superior fusion) boosts sustainability.
