# Genesis Network Expansion Plans

## Overview

The Genesis Network expands from U.S. domestic pilots into a defensive, NATO-aligned Western Hemisphere initiative ("Fortress West"), then global rollout to willing countries, and ultimately a space pivot for infinite growth (Luna/Mars/asteroids/O'Neill cylinders/Von Neumann probes). Expansion leverages self-replicating pods/kits, with LLCs adapted for local needs (70–80% direct transfer; 20–30% replacements for cultural/religious accommodations like halal/kosher processing, prayer spaces, indigenous integrations). Financials: Self-funding by 2029; revenues ramp to $500B+/year by 2050, $10T+ by 2070s (space exports). CAPEX offset 15–25% early grants; IRR 35–45%; NPV $50T+.

## 1. Western Hemisphere ("Fortress West")

NATO-aligned defensive focus: Resilient hubs for security, supply chains, and alliances. Ramp: 50–200 hubs by 2035. LLCs: 18–20 total; 14–16 transfers; 3–5 replacements (e.g., TerraFab localized for export controls).

Cultural: Indigenous/Christian/Islamic adaptations. Financials: $10–50B/year by 2035 (kits/credits).
