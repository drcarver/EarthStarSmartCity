## 5. Financial & Viability Summary

- **Incremental Revenue (Mature Network)**:  
  - $6–25B/year **per major industry vertical**  
- **Blended IRR Uplift**: +8–18%  
- **Typical Component Payback**: 1–3 years

- **Regulatory Strategy**: Favor low-friction domains (energy leasing, signage, education, efficiency services)
