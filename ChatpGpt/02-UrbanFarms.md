# Earth-Star Skyscraper Farm – Prompt 2 (2032–2045)
**Validated Master Specification – November 2025**

## Core Mission
Construct and operate a nationwide network of Skyscraper Farms capable of:
- Feeding 20,000 people per node year-round with full USDA MyPlate dietary diversity
- Achieving minimum 10× land efficiency vs. 2025 U.S. averages
- Exporting renewable electricity and water in humid climates
- Generating revenue from vitamins, cultured meat, biodegradable packaging, simple APIs, and verifiable carbon credits
- Enabling rewilding of >800 million acres by 2045

## Technical Systems
### Energy
- Anaerobic digestion of all biomass → biogas
- Optional Sabatier reactor pilot
- Standard combined-cycle methane turbines (62 % efficiency) or biogas engines
- Heat recovery façades standard on all production halls and kilns

### Water
- 98 % closed-loop recycling (Newater standard)
- Atmospheric water generators integrated into façade tiles
- Geothermal heat pumps (COP >5) for HVAC efficiency

### Animal Husbandry
- Ceramic flat-pack barns for large livestock/poultry
- Rotational grazing with virtual fencing and solar herding robots
- Begin research into bioreactor tissue agriculture to eventually replace livestock

### Pharmaceutical / Cleanroom
- ISO 5–8 ceramic cleanroom kits for vitamins, botanicals, sterile supplies
- No complex biologics/injectables until separate stainless facility post-2040

### Rewilding
- Managed rewilding of purchased farmland using native seed banks and conventional breeding herds
- No artificial wombs; species include bison, elk, etc.
