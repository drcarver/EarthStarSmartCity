# Earth-Star Smart City Complex – MHUE-50k  
**Construction start:** 2 January 2035 • **Full occupancy:** Q4 2041  
**All technology commercially proven as of November 2025**

## Executive Summary

The MHUE-50k is the first fully integrated, rail-adjacent, eventually off-grid new city built 100% with Genesis Facility ceramic tiles and modular kits. Functional equipment uses standard 2025–2035 commercial systems inside ceramic shells.

- **Site:** 2,400 ha brownfield adjacent to BNSF corridor (Brownsville, TX pilot)  
- **Total built area:** 2.85 million m² across 38 major buildings  
- **Population:** 50,000 residents + 5,000 students/workers  
- **Total project cost:** $18.4 billion (2035–2041)  
- **Public funding applied:** $7.9 billion  
- **Private capital required:** $10.5 billion  
- **IRR:** 16.8 % • **NPV @ 8 %:** $22.1 billion • **Payback:** 8.2 years
