# MHUE-50k – Executive Summary (Version 3.0 – December 2025)

**Project Start Date:** 2 January 2035  
**First Full Occupancy:** Q4 2046  
**Population:** 50,000 residents  
**Gross CapEx:** $27.4–$31.2 B  
**Effective Equity (after incentives):** $18.6–$21.1 B  
**Annual EBITDA:** $1.51–$1.68 B  
**Unlevered IRR (post-incentives):** 9.8–11.4 % (base 10.6 %)  
**NPV (6 %, 2035–2070):** $14.8 B  

**Core Mission:** Build a 50,000-resident hybrid smart city with integrated housing, education, healthcare, industry, Skyscraper Farms, regenerative agriculture, and commercial-industrial hubs using only commercially proven 2025 technology.

## Aggressive Feasibility Analysis

- **Technically achievable:** Fully compliant with 2025–2035 commercial technology.  
- **Energy & power:** Realistic net import, not export. PPA agreements critical.  
- **Legal:** All ITAR, CHIPS, BIPA, state building codes, and labor regulations met.  
- **Financial:** IRR 9.8–11.4 %, NPV $14.8 B, break-even ~2044.  
- **Operational:** Construction phased over 11 years; occupancy Q4 2046.  
- **Regulatory:** All data centers, battery factories, and farms separated per CHIPS/BIS rules.  
- **Environmental:** On-site soil-carbon sequestration, regenerative agriculture, brownfield remediation; oceanic/atmospheric interventions withdrawn.
