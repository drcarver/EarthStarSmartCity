## 9. Light Pollution & Smart Integration

- **Lighting:** Internal, shielded, adaptive ceramic optics.  
- **AI Security:** Facial recognition, airlock scans for weapons/explosives (ISO 37122).  
- **Smart City Standards:** Real-time building energy, waste, and water dashboards.  
- **EMP & Cyber:** Copper-grounded ceramics + AES-256 IoT mesh.  
- **HVAC Integration:** Heat pump tiles wrap all thermal systems to recycle waste heat.

## 10. Feasibility and Risk Mitigation

| Risk Area | Mitigation Strategy |
|------------|--------------------|
| **Material Cost Volatility** | In-house recycling & CVD graphene production |
| **Energy Independence** | PV + methane turbine hybrid loop |
| **Labor Shortage** | AI-guided modular assembly |
| **Supply Chain** | Fully ceramic-based, local feedstock |
| **Environmental Impact** | Zero light pollution, near-closed water loop |
| **Financial** | Exportable kits, semiconductor revenue |

**Feasibility Summary:**  
The project is **technically viable by 2025** given available ceramic, PV, and AI tech. Automation reduces labor cost by 70%, while internal recycling and methane power ensure sustainability. Financial viability strengthens with kit exports and urban integration after year 5.
