## 1. Executive Summary

### Mission

The **TerraWell FreeCare System**, operated by the fully independent **TerraWell Foundation (501(c)(3) nonprofit)**, provides **100% free access** to essential and quality-of-life services with **no co-pays, no means testing, and no ID ever required**.

Services include:
- Nutrient-dense food (grab-and-go, school meals, and cook-to-order food-court dining)
- Over-the-counter and generic medications
- 3D-printed durable medical equipment (DME)
- Primary, urgent, and mental health care
- Indoor therapeutic **pools, saunas, and hot tubs with biomonitoring**
- Martial arts, fitness, and open recreation
- Gaming and recreation centers
- Quiet study zones
- Supervised bus drop-off / pick-up points
- Competency-based education blocks (evenings and weekends)

All facilities are protected by **Genesis Network Security & Surveillance LLC systems** and operate as **safe, supervised community anchors** in food deserts, medically underserved areas, and disaster-response zones.

**Hours of Operation**
- Standard: **6:00 AM – 11:00 PM**
- High-need pilot sites: **24/7**

## 2. Legal & Tax Structure

- **Independent 501(c)(3)**  
  TerraWell Foundation (Delaware-incorporated) with:
  - Separate board, officers, staff, IT systems, and facilities
- **Firewall Rules**
  - No overlapping personnel, data, facilities, or governance
  - No shared decision-making
- **Donation Scope (EarthStar → TerraWell)**
  - Tangible inventory only, FMV-appraised:
    - Food
    - OTC & generic medications
    - DME (wheelchairs, CPAP accessories, braces)
    - Pool, sauna, hot-tub equipment
    - Fitness, gaming, and kitchen equipment
- **Operations Funding**
  - Public donations + reserves
  - **No government grants or contracts ever accepted**
- **Compliance**
  - Annual Form 990
  - Independent audits
  - FDA pharmacy licensing
  - HIPAA compliance
  - Local zoning, food-service, and aquatic-facility codes
