## 1. Mission & Scope

Provide **100% free access** to essential and quality-of-life services **with no co-pays, means testing, or ID requirements**, including:

- Nutrient-dense food (grab-and-go, cook-to-order, school meals)  
- OTC & generic medications  
- 3D-printed durable medical equipment (DME)  
- Primary, urgent, and mental health care  
- Therapeutic pools, saunas, and hot tubs with biomonitoring  
- Fitness, martial arts, recreation, and gaming centers  
- Study zones, supervised bus points, and competency-based education  

All facilities are **Genesis Network Security & Surveillance LLC–protected** and operate as **safe community anchors**.

**Hours:** 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM (24/7 for pilot/high-need sites)

## 3. Legal & Tax Structure

- **Independent 501(c)(3) nonprofit** (TerraWell Foundation, DE-incorporated)  
- **Firewalls:** No shared personnel, governance, data, or facilities with EarthStar  
- **Donations:** Tangible inventory only (food, meds, DME, therapy & fitness equipment)  
- **Operations Funding:** Public donations + reserves, no government grants/contracts  
- **Compliance:** Annual Form 990, independent audits, FDA, HIPAA, local codes
