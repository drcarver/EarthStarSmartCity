# Integrated Space Expansion Strategy – Earth-Star Industries

**Objective:** Accelerate space colonization as soon as U.S. Genesis hubs prove self-funding. Timeline incorporates orbital factories, lunar resource extraction, Mars/Luna colonies, and O’Neill cylinder development. Innovation Hub LLC structure ensures modularity and mitigates operational risk. Revenue and operational lessons from space are **fed back to Earth hubs** to enhance resilience, productivity, and closure efficiency.

**Transport Assumptions:** SpaceX Starship v3 used for lunar transport until lunar mass driver comes online (~Phase 2–3). Mass driver then handles bulk tile, fab, and propellant logistics.
