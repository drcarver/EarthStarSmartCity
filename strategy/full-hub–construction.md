## Overview

A **full hub** is a large-scale Genesis site (typically 500–2,500 hectares) developed as the core production and community node in the Network. It follows the 4-zone regenerative model (**Productive Core** 40%, **Residential & Community** 30%, **Infrastructure & Circulation** 15%, **Nature Corridors & Rewilding** 15%) and achieves 95–99% resource closure across food, energy, water, materials, and services.

Full hubs are built using only **[tiles](components/tiles.md)**, **[pods](components/pods.md)**, and **[kits](components/kits.md)** — with **blind-mate interconnects** and **IP67-equivalent seals** for rapid, resilient assembly. They are self-funded after bootstrap via early revenue streams (greens, tiles, biogas tipping, credits) and scale to support 50,000–600,000 residents + surplus export.

**Typical Timeline to Maturity**: 18–36 months (accelerated by pre-supply staging and automation).

## Phases & Milestones

### Phase 0: Pre-Survey & Site Selection (Months -6 to -1)
- **Activities**:
  - Identify large distressed land (brownfield, closed landfill, degraded farmland) via **[OS & Software LLC](innovation-hub/os-software-llc.md)** digital twin analysis.
  - Conduct preliminary satellite/drone survey + soil/contaminant testing.
  - Secure land (purchase/lease) + file EPA Brownfields/USDA REAP/HUD CDBG grant applications.
  - Engage local co-op/affiliates for community buy-in.
- **Milestone**: Site acquired & grants approved; initial digital twin model created.
- **Cost**: $0.5–$2M (surveys, legal, land deposit).
- **Revenue**: $0.

### Phase 1: Initial Survey, Remediation & Bootstrap Infrastructure (Months 1–6)
- **Activities**:
  - Deploy **[dense sensor net kit](components/kits/dense-sensor-net-kit.md)** + ground radar for detailed mapping.
  - Apply **[biochar & soil amendment kit](components/kits/biochar-soil-amendment-kit.md)** + **[microbial inoculant production kit](components/kits/microbial-inoculant-production-kit.md)** for bioremediation.
  - Install **[foundation/base tiles](components/tiles/foundation-base-tile.md)** and **[permeable road/path tiles](components/tiles/permeable-road-path-tile.md)** for access grid.
  - Deploy **[energy storage starter kit](components/kits/energy-storage-starter-kit.md)** + **[roof glazing BIPV tiles](components/tiles/roof-glazing-semi-transparent-bipv.md)** for power.
  - Set up **[biogas digester pods](components/pods/biogas-digester-pod.md)** and **[AWG recovery](components/energy-water-closure-systems.md)** for water/energy bootstrap.
- **Milestone**: Site remediated; basic power/water online; first revenue from tipping fees + early produce.
- **Cost**: $5–$15M (remediation + bootstrap kits/pods).
- **Revenue**: $0.5–$5M/month (tipping, greens, biogas).

### Phase 2: Core Production & First Residential Zones (Months 7–12)
- **Activities**:
  - Build **[productive core](docs/regenerative-city-layout.md)**: **[aquaponics pods](components/pods/aquaponics-pod.md)**, **[vertical ag expansion kits](components/kits/vertical-ag-expansion-kit.md)**, **[large animal pods](components/pods/large-animal-pod.md)** (cattle/sheep/goats/pigs).
  - Construct first residential zones using **[food desert housing starter kit](components/kits/food-desert-housing-starter-kit.md)** + **[smart airlock kits](components/kits/smart-airlock-kit.md)**.
  - Install **[smart display walls](components/tiles/smart-display-walls.md)** and **[basic networking snap tiles](components/tiles/basic-networking-snap-tile.md)** for connectivity.
  - Deploy **[dense sensor net kit](components/kits/dense-sensor-net-kit.md)** network-wide.
- **Milestone**: Cash-flow positive (Month 9–12); first residents housed; surplus food/energy exported.
- **Cost**: $20–$60M (production + housing pods/kits).
- **Revenue**: $5–$20M/month (produce, energy, housing rent).

### Phase 3: Full Expansion & Maturity (Months 13–36)
- **Activities**:
  - Complete all 4 zones: expand **[residential & community](docs/regenerative-city-layout.md)** with **[TerraWell FreeCare](innovation-hub/terrawell-freecare-llc.md)** centers, retail plazas, schools.
  - Scale **[infrastructure & circulation](docs/regenerative-city-layout.md)**: **[high-speed rail extensions](components/kits/hsr-rail-extension-kit.md)**, **[autonomous mobility](innovation-hub/autonomous-mobility-llc.md)** links.
  - Achieve 95%+ closure: full **[energy systems](components/energy-water-closure-systems.md)**, **[water sovereignty](innovation-hub/water-sovereignty-llc.md)**, **[nutrient cycling](innovation-hub/nutrient-cycling-llc.md)**.
  - Integrate **[smart airlock module tiles](components/tiles/smart-airlock-module-tile.md)** in all buildings; certify against **[ISO/TC 268](docs/standards/iso-tc-268-smart-cities.md)** and **[ISO 14001](docs/standards/iso-14001-environmental.md)**.
- **Milestone**: Full operational maturity — self-sufficient, exporting surplus food/energy/materials/services, supporting 50,000–200,000 residents.
- **Cost**: $50–$150M (expansion + standards certification).
- **Revenue**: $100–$400M/year (mature hub).

## Recomputed Financials (per Full Hub, Mature)

- **Total CAPEX**: $75–$225M (phased over 18–36 months; offset 20–40% by grants).
- **Break-Even**: Month 9–12 (cash-positive Month 6–9 in aggressive case).
- **Annual Revenue (mature)**: $100–$400M (food/protein 40%, energy/tiles 25%, credits 15%, services/retail 20%).
- **IRR**: 35–50% (high due to revenue stacking + low maintenance).
- **Viability Impact**: +20–30% for food-desert/urban rollout (local production + jobs reduce resistance).

## Key Milestones Summary Table

| Month Range | Phase | Milestone | Cumulative Cost | Monthly Revenue |
|-------------|-------|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| -6 to 0     | Pre-Survey | Site approved, grants filed | $0.5–$2M | $0 |
| 1–6         | Survey & Bootstrap | Remediation complete, basic power/water online | $5–$15M | $0.5–$5M |
| 7–12        | Core Production | First residents housed, cash-flow positive | $25–$75M | $5–$20M |

**Bottom Line**: Full hubs are the engine of the Genesis Network — they bootstrap revenue, house communities, and drive exponential scaling. The phased approach (survey → bootstrap → production → maturity) ensures self-funding momentum while building resilience and productivity from Day 1.
