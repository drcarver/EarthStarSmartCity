## Objective

Launch new Genesis hubs (500–2,500 ha core parcels) and affiliate mini-hubs (5–60 ha) in the **fastest credible timeline** while achieving **cash-positive operations within 3–6 months** and **self-replication capacity within 12–18 months** — with **zero long-term public funding dependence** and **maximum realistic defense against every identified catastrophic failure mode**. All tiles, pods, and kits are manufactured in Tile & Pipe Production Pods → permanent Tile Factories using 70–95% waste-derived materials. Buildings constructed using only tiles, ceramics, and kits, with mandatory smart airlocks (scanning for weapons/explosives, alerting security). Implements smart city standards (ISO/TC 268, IEEE 2030, ITU-T Y.4900) and sustainable building codes (ISO 14001, LEED/Cradle-to-Cradle).

## Core Principles (Enforced as Non-Negotiable Doctrine)

1. **Pre-Supply Dominance** — New sites receive **nearly-mature, already-producing pods + live biology + feedstock** from staging/mother hubs or Level 0.5 partners.  
2. **Staging / Mother Hub Obligation** — Mature hubs dedicate 10–20% output to seeding.  
3. **Revenue-First Pruning** — Day-1 focus: microgreens/herbs, eggs, honey, early milk/cheese, biogas tipping, first ceramic tiles.  
4. **Biology Survival Gate** — >85% viability or abort.  
5. **Permitting & Community Firewall Gate** — Strict pre-approval + buy-in mandatory.  
6. **Revenue Velocity Gate** — ≥4 pre-sold streams locked before Day 1.  
7. **Supply-Chain & Critical-Parts Gate** — Dual-sourcing + buffer enforced.  
8. **Governance & Reputation Firewall Gate** — Charter, audits, PR from Day 1.  
9. **Water & Energy Independence Gate** — ≥70% AWG + 72-hour blackout proven.  
10. **Yield Conservatism Rule** — -30% to -50% adjustments until 6-month actuals.

- **Months 10–18**: Exponential (5–20 new pods/month/cluster); mother hubs seed 10–20% output.
