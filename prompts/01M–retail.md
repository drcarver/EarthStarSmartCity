## Objective

Integrate **grocery, liquor, home & garden retail, and AI-assisted modular sensors** into Genesis hubs **with Prism module deployment, SUC lifecycle, and digital twin simulation**, while **maintaining ultra-rapid bootstrap alignment** and revenue-first prioritization.  

This prompt **supersedes and replaces** the original 01M, 02B, and related HIL/sensor prompts for Phase 1–2 hub deployment.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Ultra-Rapid Alignment** – Retail and sensor modules only after initial bootstrap gates (Month 3–6).  
2. **AI-Assisted Sensor Modules** – All hub sensors (RTK, environmental, ag, bio, security) deployed as **Prism modules**, with **partial classes, auto-test stubs, and SUC manifests**.  
3. **Digital Twin Simulation** – Use simulated pods to model **energy, water, yield, and resiliency** before physical deployment.  
4. **Phased Retail Integration** – Start high-margin produce/dairy/eggs/honey; expand to full grocery, liquor, and home & garden retail.  
5. **Sovereign & Privacy-First** – Prism host + SUC ensures modular updates, rollback, and privacy-preserving data flows.  
6. **Cultural Compliance** – Halal/kosher lines, region-specific variants.  
7. **Resource Simulation** – Climate data and digital twin outputs drive power, water, and resource planning for pods and hub modules.

## Phased Integration Strategy

### Phase 1 – Pre-Retail / Hub Bootstrap (Months 1–3)
- Focus on initial cash-positive production (tiles, mini-fabs, basic food/energy outputs).  
- Begin digital twin simulation of pods, resource usage, and sensor modules.  
- Early affiliate onboarding using basic HIL badges.  

### Phase 2 – Retail Pilot & Sensor Deployment (Months 4–9)
- Deploy AI-generated sensor modules as Prism modules (RTK, environmental, ag).  
- Pilot retail: produce/dairy/eggs/honey.  
- Digital twin validates energy, water, and workflow; SUC-enabled Prism host applies updates.  
- Early revenue: $20k–$100k/month; surplus 20–30% to TerraWell.  

### Phase 3 – Full Retail & Hub Expansion (Months 10–18)
- Deploy full retail pods (liquor, home & garden) and remaining sensor modules (bio, security).  
- Large animal and silvopasture pods integrated; all SUC-enabled for atomic updates.  
- Digital twin simulates resilience, redundancy, and climate stress.  
- Revenue: $100k–$400k/month; surplus 30–40% to TerraWell.  

### Phase 4 – Hub Completion & Scale (Month 19+)
- Full hub with all pods, tiles, and sensor modules.  
- Digital twin supports predictive maintenance, resource optimization, and SUC deployment schedules.  
- Network-wide scaling: enables mother-hub replication via on-site pod and module production.  
- Revenue: $500k–$2M/month; surplus 40% to TerraWell.
