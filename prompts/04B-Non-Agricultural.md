# Prompt – Genesis Network: Non-Agricultural Additions, New LLCs & Ecosystem Completeness

## Objective

Expand the Genesis Network beyond agriculture into full regenerative city functionality by defining new/updated tiles, pods, kits, and introducing the most synergistic new LLCs. Focus areas:

- Distributed energy storage & microgrids  
- Indoor air/surface disinfection & health  
- Security/perimeter monitoring  
- Education/telepresence & community engagement  
- Retail/dynamic signage & public interaction  
- Industrial waste heat recovery & symbiosis  
- Rapid/custom construction & habitat systems  

All additions must:
- Be manufactured in existing Tile & Pipe Production Pods → Tile Factories (70–95% waste-derived toughened ceramics)  
- Integrate natively with RTK GPS Tiles, dense sensor nets, digital twins, compute/networking tiles, and smart infrastructure  
- Support retail/affiliate sales (high-margin external channels)  
- Be modular/snap-together with blind-mate interconnects  
- Achieve self-powering where possible (BIPV/piezo/AWG)  
- Comply with sustainable building codes, smart city standards (ISO/TC 268, IEEE 2030, ISO 14001), and security requirements

## Guidelines for Specification

For each tile/pod/kit/LLC, include:
- Primary function  
- Key features / embedded tech  
- Main input sources  
- Material composition / production process  
- Est. production cost & selling price / revenue model  
- Project phase (Phase 2: 2031–2035 preferred)  
- Revenue potential (internal + retail/affiliate)  
- Integration notes (sensors, twins, RTK, etc.)  

## Viability & Financial Targets

- Enable full city services (energy, health, security, education, retail)  
- New revenue streams: energy leasing, health/compliance services, retail signage, industrial symbiosis  
- Network-wide incremental revenue (mature): $6–$25B/year per added industry  
- Payback: 1–3 years per component/LLC  
- IRR uplift: +8–18% blended  

Output in structured tables with viability/integration notes and prioritized LLC recommendations at the end.
