## Mandatory Compliance Rules (Never Violated)

- All sensor facilities are **physically and air-handling separated** from the 12–14 nm logic TerraFab (minimum 800 m) → satisfies CHIPS/BIS guardrails
- No pharmaceutical-grade sensors (DO, TAN, NO₃, qPCR) are produced in any fab that also makes military-qualifiable logic → satisfies FDA/ICH Q9 cross-contamination rules
- All high-precision optical sensors (PAR quantum, spectral) are manufactured in ISO 14644-1 Class 100–1000 cleanrooms, never in the logic fab
