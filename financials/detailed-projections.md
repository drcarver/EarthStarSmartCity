# Genesis Network Detailed Financial Projections

**Version Date:** January 2026  
**Prepared by:** EarthStar Technologies Conceptual Framework  
**License:** This work is licensed under a [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

## Assumptions & Methodology

- **Self-Funding Core**: No long-term public funds; early revenue from [**greens**](components/aquaponics.md), [**tiles**](components/tiles/roof-glazing.md), [**biogas**](components/pods/biogas-digester.md), tipping fees achieves cash-positive Month 2–6; full self-funding by 2029–2030.
- **Revenue Streams**: Diversified (60–80% margins mature): Food/produce (40%), [**Tiles**](components/tiles/exterior-cladding.md)/infrastructure (25%), Energy/biogas (15%), Credits/contracts (10%), [**Kits**](components/kits/ceramic-tile-production.md)/affiliates (10%).
- **Viability Enhancements**: Premium branding (+20–40% margins), infrastructure contracts, regenerative finance, waste heat symbiosis, [**microbial boosts**](components/kits/microbial-inoculant.md).
- **Scenarios**: Conservative (slower adoption), Base (realistic), Aggressive (optimal execution).
- **Discount Rate**: 7% for NPV; IRR calculated per phase/network.
- **Currency**: USD Billions ($B); network-wide.

## Revenue Breakdown (Mature Phase, Base Scenario, $B/year)

- Food/Produce/Processing: 40–60% ($3–9T).
- [**Tiles**](components/tiles/permeable-roads.md)/Infrastructure/Contracts: 20–30% ($2–4.5T).
- Energy/Biogas/Power Export: 10–15% ($1–2T).
- Credits/Tokens/Finance: 10–15% ($1–2T).
- [**Kits**](components/kits/agroforestry-expansion.md)/Affiliates/Electronics: 10–15% ($1–2T).

## Risk-Adjusted Notes

- **Conservative**: Slower adoption, regulatory delays.
- **Base**: Realistic execution with quick wins.
- **Aggressive**: Optimal branding/contracts/flywheel.

Self-funding remains core; early phases fund all later growth.
