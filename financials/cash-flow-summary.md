# Cash Flow Summary  
**Genesis Network Financial Projections (2027–2077)**  
**Recalculated as of January 21, 2026**

This document provides a detailed, phased cash flow summary for the **Genesis Network**, covering capital expenditures (**CAPEX**), revenue, operating expenses (**OPEX**), net cash flow, and cumulative cash reserves. Projections are conservative yet aggressive, assuming successful **self-funding** by 2028–2029 through **pod-centric bootstrap** and high-margin early outputs (greens, biogas, tiles, credits). All figures in **$B USD** (billions); ranges reflect base/best-case scenarios incorporating viability enhancements (microbial yields, blockchain credits, space synergies).

Key assumptions:  
- Early revenue from **high-margin pods** (aquaponics, livestock, tile production)  
- Scaling via **smallholder partnerships** and **city/tower density**  
- Uplifts from **NASA/ISS feedback loops** (+5–10% IRR by 2040s) and **TerraWell tax offsets** ($0.5–1.5B/year)  
- No long-term public funding; short-term grants offset 20–40% early CAPEX  

See **[CAPEX/Revenue Breakdown](capex-revenue-breakdown.md)** for component details and **[NPV/IRR Calculations](npv-irr-calculations.md)** for valuation metrics.
