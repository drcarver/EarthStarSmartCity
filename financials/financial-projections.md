# Financial Projections & Self-Funding Path

**Currency**: All figures in constant 2026 USD (inflation-adjusted internally via 2.5% annual deflator where relevant)  
**Core Assumption**: 100% self-funded after seed phase; no external equity or long-term public grants beyond one-time EPA/USDA offsets in 2027–2029.

## 4. IRR & NPV Calculation Assumptions

- **Reinvestment Rate**: 70–90% of net profit plowed back into replication (hubs, towers, space ramp)  
- **Discount Rate**: 7% (conservative long-term real rate)  
- **Terminal Value**: Assumes perpetual growth at 2–3% post-2077 (space economy maturity)  
- **NPV Range**: $3.5T–$11T (2050 horizon); $10T–$35T+ (2200 horizon with cylinders/probes)

## 6. Conclusion & Realism Note

The self-funding path is credible if early revenue flywheel (tiles + biogas + greens + credits) ignites by mid-2028. Cities/towers accelerate density-driven revenue but extend full U.S. coverage timeline by 10–20 years without public funds. The project remains high-risk/high-reward: 5–12% chance of major U.S. impact, <1% for full multi-planetary vision — but even partial success would be civilization-altering.
